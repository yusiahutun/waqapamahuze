Nation Courier Services branches, delivery, and tracking

Did you know Nation, the popular media company, has courier services? They operate under Nation Courier Services and have created a profound presence in the niche. So, what are the services offered, and how sophisticated is it?

Have you ever wondered how your favourite newspaper reaches you on time every morning? You have the amazing logistical team to thank as they work overnight to ascertain their clients read the news in the morning. Nation has proven to have one of the best delivery systems, using this infrastructure to create the courier service.

Considering the efficiency of the newspaper delivery system, you would expect the courier services, set up with such a platform, to succeed as well. And that is what Nation gives clients, a fastidious delivery system backed by years of perfection. But how does Nation Courier Services work?

What is Nation Courier Services?

Nation Courier Services is a transport unit of the Nation Media Group that transports newspapers, delivers mail, documents, gift hampers and everything else that can be transported. The company offers fast, reliable, and affordable courier services countrywide and in other parts of East Africa.

You can access Nation Courier Services in Nairobi, as the firm's primary service areas are between Nairobi and Kampala, with overnight services.

They boast the lowest rates for sending gifts and packages to different parts of the country. According to their Facebook page, one can send an item for as low as Ksh.150.

The company has also partnered with various stakeholders in the industry, including Liquor Library (Kiambu Rd branch). Liquor Library is now the company's official drop-off point where clients can easily drop off and pick up their packages.

You can always get in touch for drop off and pick up by using their official contact number, 0757 003 995. The company is gradually expanding and will soon join the list of international courier services in Nairobi.

Nation Courier tracking

Does the company provide tracking services like other logistic companies in Kenya and other parts of the world? The good thing is that they have an application you can download from the Google Play Store. You can also access your parcel's information, including tracking information, from their official website. You will have to enter your tracking number.
