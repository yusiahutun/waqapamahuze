Early Life

On the 18th of December in 1954, Raymond Allen Liotta was born in Newark, New Jersey. He was adopted by township clerk Mary Edgar and auto-parts store owner Alfred Liotta when he was only six months old.

He had a sister, who was also adopted. Apart from her, he had one biological half-brother and five biological half-sisters. In terms of his education, he graduated from Union High School in New York City in 1973.

He was a member of the school’s Hall of Fame. He then went on to study acting at the University of Miami, earning a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in 1978. While there, he appeared in musicals like Cabaret, Dames at Sea, and The Sound of Music.
