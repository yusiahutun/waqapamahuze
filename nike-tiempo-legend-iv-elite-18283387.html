Nike Tiempo Legend IV Elite - Nikefootball.com, 275

The Nike Tiempo Legend IV Elite is the redesigned version of the classic boot, as first worn by legends Paolo Maldini and Eric Cantona.

The boots, the lightest Tiempo yet created, are made from durable leather and are designed to enhance touch, control and strike.

You can see them in action on the feet of  Barcelona’s World Cup-winning defender Gerard Piqué this season.
