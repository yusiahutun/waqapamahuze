I Don't Know What Her Decision Will Be

Candy Spelling is speaking out about Tori Spelling's tumultuous relationship!

During an interview on "Oprah: Where Are They Now?," the Spelling family matriarch talks about Tori's marital problems with Dean McDermott and their complex mother-daughter relationship.

On the heels of the season finale of "True Tori," the "Beverly Hills, 90210" alum has a big decision to make about the future of her marriage.

Fans were left wondering if the actress will stay with McDermott, after a very public cheating scandal with Emily Goodhand.

"Tori hasn't really ask me for advice," Candy reveals. "I am always there for her. I will always support Tori, and I just wanna stand behind her no matter what she needs to do."

As for the current state of Tori and Dean's relationship, it looks like they're still dedicated to trying to make it all work out.

"I don't know what her decision will be," Candy adds. "I just know he's a great father and they're great together."

The 68-year-old is also opening up about her strained relationship with her daughter, which has had many ups and downs over the years.

"I am by no means saying that I am the perfect mother now or I didn't make mistakes when my children were growing up. In therapy I learned that I was passive-aggressive and that I also had poor communication skills," Candy writes in her memoir, "Candy at Last."

"Having said that, I also believe that there comes a time when you have to stop blaming your parents. I have learned from my own journey that at some point you have to take responsibility for your own actions and attitudes," she continues.

Candy is airing all of her dirty laundry!
