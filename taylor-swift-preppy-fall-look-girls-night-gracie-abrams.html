Taylor Swift Nails the Preppy Fall Look on a Girls' Night With Gracie Abrams

Taylor Swift loves a girls’ night. Moments after returning to New York City from Argentina, where she played her latest Eras Tour shows, in Buenos Aires, the singer stepped out with fellow singer-songwriter Gracie Abrams.

The “Karma” singer showed off her signature girly, preppy style in a little, slightly ruffled white minidress wore over a fitted navy blazer by Gant. She kept warm with some sheer brown tights and brown snakeskin loafers with block heels. Swift added a pop of green with a small crossbody bag with golden hardware, and pops of red, with her bright red lip and matching manicure. She wore a sharp black cat eye, and her hair was down straight.

Abrams took a more casual approach to her outfit—which involved a beige zip-up rain jacket and black straight-leg trousers—but still added hints of prep with black loafers worn with white socks and a black quilted Chanel mini bag with a gold chain strap.

DAMEBK/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Swift has been on a preppy style streak as of late. Earlier in the month, she grabbed dinner with friend Phoebe Bridgers in Manhattan’s Greenwich Village, and looked elegant in a look that involved all the autumn hues. The fit was composed of a collared orange-and-brown patterned Gucci sweater, dark brown straight-leg trousers, orange socks, and the same reddish-brown leather loafer wedges she wore last night. She finished off the look with a small structured brown purse, black winged eyeliner, and her red lip.

Also this month, Swift wore a preppy schoolgirl look for a NoHo girls’ night with her star-studded crew, including Selena Gomez, Sophie Turner, Gigi Hadid, Cara Delevingne, and Brittany Mahomes. The “Anti-Hero” singer looked sleek in a black long-sleeved top, a pleated checkered brown miniskirt cinched at the waist with a black leather belt, black thigh-high boots, and a black purse. She completed the look with a dainty gold necklace, gold earrings, and her signature black winged eyeliner and red lip.
