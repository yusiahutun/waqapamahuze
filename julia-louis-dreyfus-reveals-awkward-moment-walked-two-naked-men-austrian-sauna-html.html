Julia Louis-Dreyfus reveals the awkward moment she walked in on two naked men in an Austrian sauna

It sounds like one of the hilarious awkward plots her character Elaine Benes suffered through on Seinfeld.  

While shooting her latest film Downhill in Austria, Julia Louis-Dreyfus took time out of her busy schedule to go to the sauna.

But the 59-year-old's relaxation was cut short when she, while completely nude, walked in on two naked men already enjoying the steamy atmosphere.

Julia has gone from strength to strength since she rose to fame as a cast member on Saturday Night Live, before nabbing the role of outspoken and endlessly hilarious Elaine on Seinfeld. 

After the iconic show's conclusion in 1998, Julia went on to star in The New Adventures of Old Christine until 2010 and later Veep, which concluded last year.  

She has gone through her own personal struggles with her father and sister passing away in recent years, as well as being diagnosed with breast cancer in 2017. 

Julia is now in remission and won't be slowing down any time soon, revealing she feels she has her 'energy back', only doing projects that bring her 'joy'.
