Why do I dream I brake but my car doesn't stop?

Dreams of this kind are really very common and more often than not tell of the dreamer's feelings about being out of control. In many similar dreams the dream car is often seen to be tearing along and the dreamer cannot stop it.

Your dream, however, seems slightly different, in that you are unable to stop your dream car from quite a slow pace. Nevertheless, I still think this dream is connected to some unconscious feelings about not being able to put the brakes on something that you feel is just slowly driving away with you.

This 'something' may be a relationship, a friendship, something to do with work, career or family. But whatever it is, it is well worth trying to work out what it is because the recurring dream will continue until you really reach the bottom of this puzzle.

I hope that pedal is able to work and that you can regain some control in your life.
