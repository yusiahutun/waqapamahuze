Max Charles Age, Parents, Height, Siblings

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: August 14, 2019

Hollywood is a tough cookie to crack. Many spend their lifetime trying to make it big but end up failing miserably. However, Max Charles has emerged as a winner despite his short-run in the industry. Still a teenager, the actor has managed to establish himself as someone to keep an eye out for.

Max is best known for his role as the younger version of the iconic friendly neighborhood superhero Spiderman in The Amazing Spider-Man and for voicing the character of Sherman in Mr. Peabody & Sherman in 2014. In 2019, Max is busy with the post-production of Ali's Realm.

Bio: Age, Parents, Siblings

Born in Nashville, Tennessee in 2003, Max Charles is of American nationality. He celebrates his birthday on the 18th of August every year. A teenager, the actor resides in California with his parents.

Regarding his family, Max has three older siblings, namely Logan Charles, Mason Charles, and Brock Charles. Just like himself, his brothers have also dabbled a bit in the entertainment industry.

Max grew up in the household of Robert Martensen and Anne Carver. His father is a writer and doctor, while his mother is an activist and a philanthropist. Besides, Max also has a pet dog named Pip in the family. Max adopted the dog after he acted alongside him in Spooky Buddies.

At the age of 15, Max stands with a height of 5 feet 10 inches (1.78 m). The young actor still has much room to grow in the coming years.

Girlfriend? Is Max Dating?

Being just a young actor, Max has not had a lot in the relationship department. Focused on his studies and acting career, in 2014, Max was the subject of the talk of Hollywood. 

During the premiere of Mr. Peabody & Sherman, Max recieved a kiss from his co-actor, Ariel Winter. The pair were attending an event at the Regency Village Theatre in Westwood, California, during which the surprise kiss in the cheek blew him away.

Max Charles receives a kiss from Ariel Winter at the premiere of Mr. Peabody & Sherman in 2014 (Photo: justjaredjr.com)

Many speculated that the young pair were boyfriend and girlfriend, but there were no more advancements in the matter after the incident.

Career In Movies & TV Shows

Max Charles began his acting journey at the age of 6 when he became a part of the third season of the popular HBO series, True Blood. In 2010, Max appeared on Raising Hope, the USA Network sitcom which aired successfully from 2010 to 2014.

Meanwhile, Max had made his film debut through the 2010 CBC TV movie, November Christmas.

Since then, he has acted in movies like Scent of the Missing, Unstable, The Three Stooges, and The Amazing Spider-Man, which he followed up with The Amazing Spider-Man 2 in 2014.

Max got his first lead role on The Neighbors, the American television fiction sitcom in 2012. For his part, Max was nominated for the in multiple categories and even won the Outstanding Young Ensemble on TV Series.

In 2015, the actor was highly praised for his appearance in the animated TV series, Harvey Beaks. Later in 2016, he lent his voice to the character Bobby in The Angry Birds Movie and played a voice role the following year in the direct-to-video film, Scooby-Doo! Shaggy Show.

Max has also made recurring contributions to the popular TV show Family Guy since 2012. In 2018, he voiced the character of a teenage boy in the Pal Stewie episode.

Although what the young actor gets paid for his time is not known, he earns more than the average of $50K. Max's early start in the industry will help him in the future.
