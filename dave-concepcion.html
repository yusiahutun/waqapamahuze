Dave Concepcion is a famous Baseball Player. He was born on June 17, 1948 and his birthplace is Venezuela. Dave is also well known as, 9-time MLB All-Star shortstop who anchored the infield for Cincinnati’s Big Red Machine in the 1970s.

Dave is originated from Venezuela. He played alongside Pete Rose when the Cincinnati Reds won back-to-back World Series titles in the mid-1970s.
