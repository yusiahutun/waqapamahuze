Kylie Jenner Wears Tiny Balenciaga Mini Dress and Neon Slip That You Can Still Shop

  • Kylie Jenner celebrated the fact that it's finally the weekend by wearing an asymmetric crushed-velvet mini dress by Balenciaga in her latest Instagram video.
  • The Kylie Cosmetics founder regularly wears mini dresses and isn't afraid to showcase a naked look.
  • Just last month, Jenner rocked a $16,900 orange naked dress by Julien Macdonald, which was basically a wardrobe malfunction waiting to happen.

If you've been ready for the weekend since Monday, then you're going to relate super hard to Kylie Jenner's latest post.

Sharing a video of herself walking towards the car on her driveway, Jenner's caption celebrated the fact that Friday night had finally arrived.
