A New Nest For Toronto

TORONTO (CelebrityAccess) — Nest, a new Toronto nightclub located at 423 College St. is set to debut next month.

The newly renovated, 13,000 square foot, 300-capacity venue is opening next month with debut performances by South African house music producer Black Coffee on Friday, May 15; Norwegian breakout artist Fehrplay on Saturday, May 16; and NYC dance music pioneer Danny Tenaglia on Sunday, May 17.

The club is being lead by a team consisting of veteran nightlife operator and BPM director Vito Tomasicchio, who operates the Digital Dreams Festival, as well as Kiwe, Maison, Century Room, Li'ly, The Crest and Nodo; BPM co-founder, director and Blue Parrot Beach Club co-owner Phillip Anthony Pulitano; and RYZE owner Rob Oliverio.

BPM's director of marketing and No.19 Music label co-owner Nitin Kalyan will handle bookings and marketing at the club.

“We are bringing our collective experiences from past projects and expertise from operating eight editions of the BPM Festival to the NEST nightclub experience. There has been so much talent that we’ve witnessed in Mexico that we were truly inspired and excited to have a platform to showcase them on a weekly basis in our home city,” – Nest co-owner Vito Tomasicchio. – Staff Writers
