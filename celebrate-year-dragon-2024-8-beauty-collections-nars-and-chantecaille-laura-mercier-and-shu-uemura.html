Celebrate the Year of the Dragon 2024 with 8 beauty collections, from Nars and Chantecaille to Laura

Nars has released a collection of its bestselling products in limited edition packaging decorated with Lunar New Year motifs. Whether you’re starting off your radiant year with the exclusive Powermatte Lipstick in Start Me Up or the brand’s new Quad Eyeshadow, get ready for a year of looking flawless.

6 sneakers to step out in for Chinese New Year 2024, the Year of the Dragon

2. Chantecaille

Chantecaille, a renowned botanical skincare and cosmetics brand, has unveiled two products to celebrate the Year of the Dragon. While the Bio Lifting Serum+ visibly smooths out wrinkles and uplifts your complexion, the Lip Chic in Red Juniper completes your look with a lightweight yet vibrant shade of red to your lips.

3. Rituals Cosmetics

Inspired by the ancient tales of Chinese imperial dragons, The Legend of the Dragon is Rituals Cosmetics’ latest limited edition collection that is sure to enchant you. Body scrub, shower foam and more are infused with the scents of plum and cedar wood, which will transform your daily body care routine.

4. Laura Mercier

The Laura Mercier limited edition Year of the Dragon collection has reimagined and packaged coveted favourites in red and gold intricate designs including the iconic translucent loose setting powder, mini RoseGlow Caviar Stick Duo in shades Wild Rose and Bed of Roses, as well as a Blush Colour Infusion in Ginger.

Chinese horoscopes: what to expect in the Year of the Wood Dragon 2024

5. Diptyque

Featuring bold artwork by Chinese calligrapher Ao Hei, Diptyque celebrates with the brand’s first-ever limited edition L’Eau Papier. The eau de toilette is a blend of white musk, mimosa, blonde wood and rice steam.

6. Shu Uemura

Shu Uemura embraces the allure of the Year of the Dragon with its Invincible Reds collection that bridges the gap between tradition and modernity. Featuring a calligraphic dragon design across the limited edition packaging, the dragon symbolises charisma and success.

7. Fresh

Want to step up your skincare regimen? Fresh unveils an exquisite Lunar New Year collection that pays homage to the Year of the Dragon with vibrant dragon totem packaging. The limited edition series comprises three products that features the brand’s iconic tea-infused formulas, including the Kombucha Facial Treatment Essence, Tea Elixir Skin Resilience Activating Serum and Black Tea Advanced Age Renewal Cream.

Gucci’s Chinese New Year capsule collection for the Year of the Dragon 2024

8. Clé de Peau Beauté

Clé de Peau Beauté introduces its Lunar New Year collection with a captivating collaboration with young artist Xi Wen, who brings her imaginative interpretation of the dragon to life. Level up your beauty game with either its bestselling Le Sérum or Radiant Cushion Foundation in a limited edition case.
