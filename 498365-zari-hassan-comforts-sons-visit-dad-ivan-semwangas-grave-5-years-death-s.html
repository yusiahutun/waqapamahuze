Zari Hassan's kids got emotional while visiting their father Ivan Semwanga's grave in Kayunga District, Uganda.

Ivan Semwanga's sons teary

Ivan was buried in May 2017, and Zari, together with their sons Pinto, Raphael and Quincy attended his funeral. Their children were still young, and the firstborn was barely 13.

Recently, the three all-grown boys visited their dad's grave, and it was an emotional moment for them. In a video shared by Zari, the boys fought tears as they looked at their dad's gravesite and those of their relatives buried next to him.

"It is well, sons," Zari captioned the video.

Zari and lover visit Ivan's gravesite

In January, TUKO.co.ke reported that Zari visited Ivan's gravesite accompanied by her lover Shakib and her other male friends. She laid wreaths on the grave and sat on top for sometime before leaving.

A section of netizens termed Shakib's presence at Ivan's gravesite 'disrespectful and shameful'. Others defended Shakib, saying there wasn't anything bad with him accompanying Zari to Ivan's gravesite.

The 42-year-old socialite has made it a tradition to visit her ex-husband's gravesite whenever she is in Uganda.
