He was raised in Goshen, Indiana and Chicago, Illinois. After his parents’ divorce, Eileen took both brothers to England. Tate attended Halyard High School and then Luton Sixth Form College. Andrew Tate is a former American-British professional kickboxer, media personality, and businessman.

Career

In November 2008, Tate was ranked the seventh-best light heavyweight kickboxer in the UK by the International Sports Kickboxing Association (ISKA). He participated in 31 kickboxing matches and had 23 wins and eight losses. His kickboxing nickname was King Cobra.
