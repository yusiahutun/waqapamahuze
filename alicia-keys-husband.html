Who’s Alicia Keys’ husband?

Alicia Keys’ husband is producer and DJ, Swizz Beatz, whose real name is Kasseem Daoud Dean. Alicia and Swizz—who have known each other since they were teenagers—started dating soon after Swizz’s split from his first wife, Mashonda Tifrere, whom he filed for divorce from in May 2010. (Swizz and Mashonda married in 2004 and share one child, a son named Kasseem Dean Jr., who was born in December 2006. Swizz also has a son named Prince Nasir Dior from his relationship with ex Nicole Levy, as well as a daughter named Nicole with ex Jahna Sebastian.)

Alicia and Swizz announced their engagement and that they were expecting their first child in May 2010. They married on July 31, 2010, in a private wedding in Corsica in the Mediterranean Sea. They welcomed their first child, a son named Egypt Daoud Dean, in October 2010. Their second son, Genesis Ali Dean, was born in 2014.

In an interview with The Wall Street Journal in 2023, Alicia opened up about the secret to her and Swizz’s happy marriage. “Really carving out the time for each other,” she said. “We all struggle with it. There’s so much to balance. There’s so much to do. And you find yourself running and doing the things for school, for the kids, and doing all the things for your lists, for all the dreams you have in your career, and what you have to do next.”

She continued, “That time together with you and your partner is so priceless. And to carve out that time and to make that important, as important as those daily things, I think it really pours back into the relationship… You remember why you are in love, you remember why you just like each other, what’s interesting about each other.”

In her memoir, More Myself , Alicia explained how she and Swizz met and started dating each other after both growing up in the Bronx, New York. “We ended up in the same circles at various awards shows and industry events,” she wrote. “He’d usually pull up in some fancy car, rolling deep with his Bronx crew and shining with diamonds. I wanted nothing to do with that scene or with Swizz.”

She continued, “The more we talked the more I noticed how his mind worked. Beyond the baggy jeans and arm tats there was depth. He was so aware of what was happening around the world.”

For more about Alica Keys, check out her 2020 memoir, More Myself: A Journey . The bestselling book—which is described as “part autobiography, part narrative documentary”—takes readers through Keys’ life and career, from her childhood in Hell’s Kitchen and Harlem, New York, to her rise to fame as one of the most celebrated musicians in the world. “In More Myself, Alicia shares her quest for truth―about herself, her past, and her shift from sacrificing her spirit to celebrating her worth,” the publisher’s description reads.
