Kentexx Cargo Kenya - reviews and FAQ

Kentex Cargo is a shipping company that enables Kenya to transport anything from the United States to Kenya by sea or by air. Kentex Cargo Kenya has received a lot of positive reviews from users with most people expressing satisfaction. There are a few complaints regarding the charges, but this is due to the confusion between actual weight and volumetric weight. The difference is that bulky products (volumetric/dimensional weight) may cost more when shipped by air. Also, the higher weight of the two is used when pricing. Besides the little complaints, Kentex prides itself on a 100% delivery record.

Location

Kentex Cargo is located in Dallas Texas. The company also has warehouses in Houston, Texas, Oklahoma City, Kansas City, Memphis Tennessee and Birmingham, Alabama. They are looking to get agencies in Boston & San Jose, California. The advantage is that Kentex Cargo can organize sea shipping from 48 states.

Kentex Cargo also ships all types of items including tractors and second-hand clothing. Their services go beyond shipping to include advice on the right products, assistance with paperwork, outsourcing, clearance, and delivery.

The procedure on their website involves three simple phases.

Phase 1: Shop.

Shop online for anything that you need to get. Create a list of all links to the items you bought.

Send the link to Kentex Cargo’s email address sales@kentexcargo.com

Phase 2: Purchase

Sending the links to Kentex is the first step of the purchase

They will go through your list and send you a quote for all the items you selected.

You can go through the invoice and accept or decline.

Once you accept, create a password for the customer portal where you can view all the details about your invoices, purchase history and modes of payment.

You will then receive the actual invoice which you will pay immediately.

This invoice does not include shipping costs as you will pay for shipping once you get the product.

Phase 3: Shipping.

You will receive a purchase confirmation with a receipt from the site you shopped.

You will also be notified once your product is shipped by either air or sea (you select the mode of transport).

Once your goods arrive, you can schedule either a pickup or a delivery. Deliveries will cost you extra charges because it is an additional service. Delivery services also apply to any part of the country through the courier service by G4S

Kentex Cargo Kenya is insured and has the necessary resources to ensure a smooth purchase and shipping process.

Kentex Cargo Rates

The cost of shipping varies depending on the mode of transport and the size or weight of your order. Shipping by air costs $15 per kilogram. The more the things you order, the more you will pay for shipping. However, the price could reduce if the weight is high, but the product is not bulky. Bulky products will cost more than less bulky products when you transport them by air.

Shipping via sea costs $28 per Cubic foot. Since the measurement is based on size, it might cost you less if you transport a lot of things. There is also the option of sharing a container which will cost you less. Additionally, if you have large volumes, Kentex will customize a container for you at an agreed price.

Shipping by air takes 10 business days while shipping by see takes about 8 weeks. The shipping costs are also inclusive of duties and other government charges.

Kentex Cargo offers free shipping on sea items for orphans and the disabled for volumes up to 10 Cubic Feet.

Kentex Cargo Nairobi

Kentex has three physical locations in Nairobi. The sea freight warehouse is located opposite the Jomo Kenyatta International Airport entrance along Mombasa road. Kentex Cargo Kenya also has offices at Corner House and in Westlands Area. This means that you can make inquiries and complaints directly if you are in Nairobi. The physical location also adds to the credibility of the company.
