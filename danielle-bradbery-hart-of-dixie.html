Danielle Bradbery to Appear on 'Hart of Dixie,' Appropriately

Perhaps the title of Danielle Bradbery's newest single 'The Heart of Dixie' was a foreshadowing into her future, because the 17-year-old singer is scheduled to appear on an upcoming episode of the CW drama 'Hart of Dixie.' Pretty appropriate, don't you think?

Fresh off her recent win on 'The Voice,' Bradbery will appear in the eighth episode of the third season, performing her debut single 'The Heart of Dixie,' which hit No. 58 on the Billboard Hot 100 and No. 16 on the Billboard U.S. Country Songs chart.

It's an exciting time for Bradbery, who just released the music video for her popular track, which gives a simple and sweet glimpse into the past. Fans love seeing the young star on camera -- the clip has had over one million views and is still in its infancy.

Television isn't all that's in the works for Bradbery -- she's also eying a November release for her first album after carefully picking songs.
