Who is Edwina Mountbatten?

Edwina was born on November 28, 1901, in London, United Kingdom. She was the elder daughter of Wilfrid Ashley and Amalia Mary Maud Cassel. Her father, Wilfrid, was a Conservative member of Parliament. Edwina's parents divorced, and her father remarried in 1914.

After her father remarried, she was enrolled in boarding schools. Edwina was unhappy during her schooling because she was bullied at school and had a sour relationship with her stepmother. Her grandfather, Sir Ernest, invited her to live with him.

She acted as hostess at his London residence and other mansions, which later became part of her inheritance. Unfortunately, Edwina is no more. She died from unknown causes on February 21, 1960, at the age of 58.

Who is Edwina Mountbatten's husband?

Edwina was married to Lord Louis Mountbatten. The pair first met in 1920. At that time, both of them were in their early 20s. Even though they had known each other only a few months, they were determined to spend their lives together. They said their marriage vows on July 18, 1922.
