Zendaya and Tom Holland Have Discussed Marriage

Tomdaya fans, it’s your lucky day — after years of rumors (and a few debunkings, more on that later) wedding bells may be in the future for Zendaya and Tom Holland...if new reports are to be believed.

According to People, the couple have had discussions about taking their 6 year relationship to the next level by getting married. "There has been talk of marriage, and that is a reality," a source told the outlet.

Should Tomdaya tie the knot, it's unlikely that fans will know right away given how little they discuss their relationship. "They are not the kind of stars who put their lives together out there on social media for the most part," the source explained, later adding that "both are private" and that being in the public eye "has never been easy or comfortable for either of them."

Zendaya and Tom Holland have been the subject of marriage rumors before. Back in 2023, the Euphoria star sent fans into a frenzy when she was posted a photo of herself to her Instagram Story, in which she wore a large ring on her engagement finger. The actress didn’t hesitate to set the record straight, taking to Instagram to debunk the engagement rumors.

"I can't post anything, you guys," Zendaya later said in a video. "I posted it for my hat… Like, not for the ring on my right finger, you guys. Seriously, you think that's how I would drop the deuce? Like, what?!"

In the meantime, Zendaya and Holland are enjoying where they are at. Speaking with Vogue, she gushed about Holland and gave insight into what being together amidst his shift in fame has been like.

“We were both very, very young, but my career was already kind of going, and his changed overnight," she explained. "One day you’re a kid and you’re at the pub with your friends, and then the next day you’re Spider-Man."

"I definitely watched his life kind of change in front of him," she added. "But he handled it really beautifully."
