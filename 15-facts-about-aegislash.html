Aegislash is a Steel/Ghost-type Pokémon.

Aegislash is a unique Pokémon known for its dual typing, which gives it an advantage against several different types of Pokémon. Its Steel typing provides excellent defense, while its Ghost typing grants it special abilities and moves.

Aegislash evolves from Doublade at level 45.

Doublade, the pre-evolved form of Aegislash, evolves into Aegislash when it reaches level This evolution unlocks Aegislash’s full potential and adds power to its already formidable arsenal of moves.

Aegislash can change forms during battle.

A unique feature of Aegislash is its ability to change between Shield Forme and Blade Forme during battles. In Shield Forme, Aegislash prioritizes defense and can withstand powerful attacks. In Blade Forme, Aegislash focuses on offense and unleashes devastating strikes.

Aegislash has the ability to learn the move King’s Shield.

King’s Shield is a signature move of Aegislash. When used, Aegislash protects itself from enemy attacks and lowers the opponent’s Attack stat. This move is highly valuable in strategic battles and can turn the tide in Aegislash’s favor.

Aegislash has a unique ability called Stance Change.

Stance Change is Aegislash’s exclusive ability, which allows it to change forms after attacking or using certain moves. It transforms Aegislash from Shield Forme to Blade Forme, enhancing its offensive capabilities and making it a formidable opponent.
