Megan Fox's devilish new look catches attention for all the right reasons

Megan Fox has switched up her hairstyle again, as she steps out with fiancé Machine Gun Kelly on a date in New York City.

Known for her long dark locks, movie star Megan Fox, stepped out in a fiery bright red wig, months after first debuting her lighter red hair, and fans are living for it.

Megan Fox steps out with bright red bob

Megan Fox, who has been keeping a low profile online since announcing the release of her new book, the 37-year-old debuted her fiery red bob with bangs, as she stepped out arm-in-arm with fiance MGK.

The Hollywood star wore a simple black polka dot dress and loafers, as her devilish red hair did all the talking. She added bling around her neck with gold necklaces around her neck, as the Bloody Valentine singer opted for silver.

MGK, whose real name is Colson Barker stepped out with his bleach blonde hair, wearing a neutral ribbed tank top with khaki pants.

The outing comes after Entertainment Tonight reported the two were “fully back together” and in a “really good place” after months of rumors they were ‘taking a break’. In July, the two went ‘Insta official’ again as MGK showed his love for Megan with a steamy Instagram comment.

Fans call the new look ‘perfect’

Upon seeing their fave step out with her new look, fans took to X, formerly known as Twitter, to express their appreciation for the star.

“PERFEITAAAAAAAAAAA,” exclaimed one.

“SHE IS SO BEAUTIFUL,” screamed another.

Of course, Machine Gun Kelly was also getting attention from fans as one joked: “they raised me”.

“They both look hot!” wrote another.

Megan debuted red hair at the Vanity Fair Oscars party

Although not as bright as the new look, Megan first made a drastic hair transformation from red to black during the 2023 Vanity Fair Oscars party, which she attended without her fiance.

At the start of March, her hairstylist posted the look and explained how he achieved such a drastic change, and fans were “obsessed”.

At the time of the post, some fans questioned if the lighter look meant she was on the journey to blonde, however, her stylist replied: “not that I know of.”

It seems like now, in Megan’s world, redheads are having more fun, and we are living for it.
