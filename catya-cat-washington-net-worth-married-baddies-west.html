Get To Know Catya Cat Washington Net Worth, Married, Baddies

Once a tomboy Catya “Cat” Washington, AKA The Elite Player, changed her ways of living a glamorous and girly lifestyle with time. She dated rappers and celebrities and appeared in Oxygen’s Bad Girls Club in Season 5. She left during episode 6 claiming that she felt too classy for the show. A lot happened to her life in between until more recently she crossed paths with 2023’s Baddies West, a Zeus Network series that premiered on 22 January 2023.

Ever since the show launched for the first time in ATL in 2021, fans had been looking forward to another season. So, the new season is here and Catya happens to be one of the newcomers. Now, let us tell you all that you all have been wondering about her.

Catya “Cat” Washington On Baddies West

Baddies West returned with the big bad tour bus and even “badder” Baddies including Catya “Cat” Washington as a newcomer.

Based on the show’s format, in this western leg, Catya, and the other ladies will be performing and hosting at some of the most “lit” clubs the cities have to offer. Of course, they will also be seen testing their patience and friendships with one another.

The network and its executive producer, Natalie Nunn promised and teased earlier that the wild west just got a whole lot wilder. But, we as the audience will have to wait and watch to see if that really happens.

If you don’t already know, Catya was sentenced to house arrest just a few days after her exit from Bad Girls Club in 2010. This happened after she was alleged of possession of cocaine, mushrooms, ecstasy, and a gun during a large drug bust in Pennsylvania. Next year, she was again placed back on house arrest. She was at the time released from a Philadelphia jail after spending a few days behind bars for violating her former probation.

Is Catya “Cat” Washington Married?
