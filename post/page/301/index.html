Galesburg parks and rec director to retire

published on May 09, 2024
Galesburg’s parks and recreation director, Elizabeth Varner, is set to retire from the city for the second time on Friday, Feb. 2. She has devoted over 33 years of service to the city of Galesburg, serving as a recreation supervisor for over 30 years before her first retirement in 2018. In 2022, Varner returned from retirement to lead the department as the director. Varner holds a bachelor’s degree in Parks and Recreation Administration from Indiana University and is a member of the Illinois Parks and Recreation Association, as well as the National Parks and Recreation Association.
