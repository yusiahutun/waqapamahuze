The Rock Island-Milan School District Early Childhood Program is now accepting Preschool For All (PFA) and Head Start applications for fall 2024, a news release says.
 
Any family who lives in the district boundary can apply. There is no cost to parents. Parents/guardians can click here to fill out the application.
 
If families have any questions they can contact 309-793-5928.
