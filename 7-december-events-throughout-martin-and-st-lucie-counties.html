7 December Events Throughout Martin And St. Lucie Counties

‘THE NUTCRACKER’

The Florida Arts & Dance Company will present the much-loved holiday classic of “The Nutcracker” from December 14 to 16 at The Lyric Theatre. A shortened presentation will be offered Sunday for those who are sensory-sensitive or for those wanting to introduce young children to the theater. (Ticket prices vary; fladance.org/events)

MAKE A DIFFERENCE

Place of Hope is looking for locals to donate gifts to support foster children for the holiday season. Make a difference through individual donations or by housing a Holiday Hope Tree in your company’s office with hope tags. You can even provide festive memory experience gifts like a trip to the movies or museum, a beach day basket and a nail salon gift certificate. (By donation; placeofhope.com/christmas)

TASTE OF JENSEN

Sample food and beverages from 25 eateries in a festive holiday atmosphere during Taste of Jensen Beach on December 11. Participants can redeem their menu ticket at the Jensen Beach Chamber of Commerce office located next to Jan’s Place Restaurant downtown. ($35; jensenbeachflorida.info)

HOLIDAY HOME TOURS

Port St. Lucie Christmas House

From December 4 to 18, the Port St. Lucie Christmas House—home of J.W. Salveson and Jerome Sangalli—will offer festive tours nightly. View more than 70 decorated trees while supporting local charities. ($10; 772.353.5517)

Old Colorado Inn

Mary’s Shelter has partnered with the Old Colorado Inn and local designers to decorate the four historic guest homes of the inn in downtown Stuart with Christmas decorations. Tour the homes December 8 and 9, and enjoy entertainment and food. ($45; maryssheltertc.org)

GFWC Woman’s Club of Stuart

Tour six of Stuart’s most elegant homes during the 10th annual GFWC Woman’s Club of Stuart Holiday Home Tour taking place on December 2. Travel between stops by car or by VIP buses. A wine and cheese reception at the clubhouse will conclude the night. (Tickets from $45; gfwcwomansclubofstuart.com)
