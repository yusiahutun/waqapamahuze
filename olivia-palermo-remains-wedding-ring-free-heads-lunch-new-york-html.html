She is known for being a style maven who wears top designer creations before they have even gone down the runway, but seems Olivia Palermo is still on the waiting list for one very important accessory.

Despite being married for almost a month, the 28-year-old star still was not wearing a wedding ring when she stepped out on New York on Monday.

Not that there is much room on her finger thanks to her giant yellow diamond engagement ring given to her by now-husband Johannes Huebl.

Out and about: Olivia Palermo enjoyed a stroll and lunch in New York on Monday

However, if not having that special band yet is bothering The City star, Olivia certainly did not show it as she trotted along Manhattan's streets with a friend.

Heading to grab a bite with yet another gal pal, the blogger looked stylish as usual in a relaxed summer ensemble.

For her day out, the reality star donned a long sheer floral skirt - under which she wore black shorts - with a blush top featuring shoulder cut outs.

Summer style: The 28-year-old donned a long sheer floral skirt with a blush top featuring shoulder cut outs

Of course, it is not what you wear but how you accessorise, so Olivia made sure to finish her look with some impressive added extras including a gold belt, sparkly flats, clear Westward Leaning frames and a stunning blush leather tote by Smythson.

As it was a casual girls' lunch, the star did not fuss over her hair, tying it back in a low ponytail.

At lunch Olivia and her friend appeared in deep conversation, with the star listening intently as her pal chatted away.

Fine diamond jewellery expert Lewis Malka said of the star's engagement band: ‘Olivia has a lovely, vivid yellow diamond engagement ring, otherwise known as canary yellow, flanked by two trapezium cut white diamonds on the shoulders.

'You can see from the image that when in the sunshine the light reflects beautifully on the stone and the intensity of the yellow is magnificent. It looks like it's nothing smaller than a four carat, radiant cut diamond and I'd estimate it's value at £300,000.’

You would think the New York fashionista would be doing all the talking after her recent nuptials.

Relaxed look: As it was a casual girls' lunch, the star did not fuss over her hair, tying it back in a low ponytail

The fashion star tied the knot with her German model husband on June 28 in Bedford, New York.

Shying away from the traditional, The City star wore a cream jumper with ostrich feathers, a white tulle skirt and matching shorts all by Carolina Herrera instead of a classic wedding dress, adding cobalt Manolo Blahniks to act as her 'something blue.'

The couple then very fittingly headed to Paris Fashion week for their honeymoon.
