Career and Professional Life

Ian is one of the American musical artists. American dubstep artist Ian Slider, better known by the stage name Space Laces, uses sound design extensively in his music. He rose to fame in 2013 as a result of his work on the Destroid project with Excision & Downlink, although he had already established his reputation before he founded Laces as a result of his Newgrounds music releases under the name corn and beans.

Between 2006 and 2013, Ian posted a lot of house and electro music under this moniker. His music was used in a variety of animations and video games, and he received support from many Newgrounds users. The primary menu theme for Castle Crashers Online was also written by Ian.

In 2010, Ian decided that he was sick of being a Newgrounds sensation and that he wanted to break into the commercial electronic music sector. He created the brand name “Space Laces” and made his debut on Houserecordings with the single “Deep Space.”

Following that, Ian received support from several small-time house musicians, including Porter Robinson and Lazy Rich, who assisted in his continued development. Ian and Figure worked together to remix Asser’s Evil. Additionally, Space remixed the songs Ho’s & Disco’s by Lucky Date and Torture by Figure, the latter of which was officially released by Big Fish Recordings.

In general, Space Laces remained quiet in 2020, however, Ian published his third Vaultage mix on March 11, 2021. The 20-minute set featured 21 unreleased songs, making it his longest to date. The first track in the mix, Dominate, also saw an official release later that month.
