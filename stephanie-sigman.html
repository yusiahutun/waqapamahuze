Biography

Stephanie Sigman was born in Ciudad Obregon, Mexico on February 28, 1987. Television and film actress best known for her roles as Estrella in the 2015 Bond film Spectre and as Valeria Velez in Netflix’s 2015 series Narcos. She starred in Spectre as one of the Bond Girls opposite Daniel Craig and Christoph Waltz. Her first major role was in the 2011 action film Miss Bala, where she played the main character. She also starred in the FX series The Bridge alongside Diane Kruger.
