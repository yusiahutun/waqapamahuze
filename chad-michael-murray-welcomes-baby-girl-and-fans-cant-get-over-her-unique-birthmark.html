Chad Michael Murray welcomes baby girl and fans can't get over her unique birthmark

Chad Michael Murray and his wife Sarah Roemer have given birth to baby number three, a baby girl, and they announced her arrival via social media by showing her adorable ‘unique’ birthmark.

The Hollywood stars welcomed their baby daughter last week, which marks their second girl, and third child, after meeting almost ten years ago on the set of the Crackle TV series, Chosen.

Chad Michael Murray and Sarah Roemer welcome baby no. 3

Taking to Instagram, Chad’s wife, Sarah Roemer, posted a picture of their daughter’s birthmark to announce her arrival, which is an adorable little red heart on her ankle.

In the caption of the post, the actress penned: “Our baby girl arrived last week with a heart on her ankle. We are so in love! Soaking up all the baby squishy moments that go by all too fast.”

The One Tree Hill actor reposted the picture onto his story, for his almost 2 million followers to see. In the caption, he wrote: “Heart full. She’s here.”

Fans congratulate the couple on their new arrival

Of course, fans rushed to the comments to congratulate the couple on their third child, as well as to comment on how adorable her birthmark is.

One wrote: “How precious that birthmark is!!! ☹️ congrats to you both!! welcome baby.”

Another fan even had an adorable suggestion, as they commented: “Congratulations!!! You should get a matching tattoo birthmark as her in the same spot.”

Others could also relate to the cute mark: “My daughter has this exact same mark on her cheek, congratulations,” wrote another.

“She’s like a Care Bear!” another exclaimed.

Chad and Sarah’s relationship explored

The pair met all the way back in 2014 filming the Crackle TV series Chosen, and the rest, as they say, is history.

Just a year later in 2015, it was confirmed the two had gotten married secretly, whilst expecting their first child, a baby boy, who was born in May.

In 2017, the celebrity couple became parents for the second time, this time, they welcomed their first daughter, and six years later, welcomed baby number three.
