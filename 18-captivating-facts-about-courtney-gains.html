The Early Years of Courtney Gains

Courtney Gains, born on August 22, 1965, in Los Angeles, California, is an American actor who has captured the hearts of many with his remarkable talent. With his captivating presence and undeniable charisma, Gains quickly established himself as a rising star.

A Breakthrough Role in “Children of the Corn”

One of the most remarkable moments in Courtney Gains’ career came in 1984 when he landed the role of Malachai in the horror film “Children of the Corn.” Gains’ portrayal of the sinister cult member gained him widespread recognition and critical acclaim, forever cementing his place in the minds of horror movie fans.
