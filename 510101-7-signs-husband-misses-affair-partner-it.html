8 signs your husband misses his affair partner after ending it

Infidelity has a devastating impact on any marriage. It leaves the betrayed partner picking up the pieces of shattered trust and rebuilding their sense of security. But what happens when the affair ends? While many couples work through the aftermath of cheating and move on, others find the effects lingering long after the affair ends. So, what are the signs your husband misses his affair partner?

When your husband ends an affair, it's natural to hope he'll fully commit to your marriage and leave the infidelity behind. However, even after the affair has ended, the emotional attachment to the affair partner might be difficult to shake.

Signs that your husband might struggle to let go of his affair partner may start showing. So, how do you know your husband still has feelings for an affair partner?

Discovering that one partner has been cheating is a scenario that many couples dread. While the initial pain of the affair may eventually subside, it's not uncommon that, as a betrayed partner, you worry that your husband is still in love with his affair partner, even after the infidelity ends.

If you suspect he is struggling with residual feelings for his former lover, there are several signs to look out for. They could indicate he is still missing his lover. Here are the 8 signs:

1. He becomes suddenly withdrawn or distant

Have you noticed your husband suddenly being more introverted? If he seems less interested in spending time with you than he used to, it could indicate that he is struggling with his emotions and missing his affair partner.

If he truly longs for his lover, he may seem less engaged in conversations with you, mostly preoccupied with his thoughts or avoiding eye contact. A man who is trying to avoid facing his emotions or the reality of the situation will start spending more time alone or away from home.

2. He talks about her frequently

Whether during an argument or randomly, your husband shouldn't talk about his affair partner, knowing it would hurt you.

If he is still bringing up his affair partner in your conversations, it could be a painful sign he is still thinking about her, and it may leave you wondering, "How long does it take for man to forget his affair partner?"

Having invested his emotions in the affair partner may cause nostalgic feelings now and then.

He may mention her name or something related to the ended relationship in a casual conversation or when talking about certain events or places that remind him of her. This can be painful for the betrayed spouse, as it may feel like the mistress is still present in the marriage.

3. He keeps mementoes or reminders of his lover

Another sign that husband still has feelings for affair partner is if you notice that your husband still has pictures, messages or gifts that remind him of his lover.

4. He seems less interested in physical intimacy with you

This affects a large percentage of any married man with multiple mistresses. And if your husband is showing less interest in physical intimacy with you, it's a possible sign he is still processing his emotions related to the ended affair. There are two possible explanations for such behaviour.

He may feel guilty or ashamed about his actions and struggle to be affectionate with his spouse. Or he may be comparing his past experiences with his affair partner to his experiences with you and finding them lacking. If the latter is the case, he is missing her/him, making it difficult to connect physically with you.

5. He becomes more secretive with his phone or computer

If your spouse starts guarding his phone or computer more closely, leaving it switched off without a good reason, it is a warning sign he is hiding something related to his affair partner. If you access his devices, he may be worried that you will discover something incriminating, like communicating with his lover.

This behaviour erodes trust in the marriage, making it difficult to rebuild a sense of security and openness simply because your husband is missing and talking to his affair partner.

6. He becomes defensive or irritable when you bring up the affair issue

If your husband is fully committed to your marriage, he will listen and try to understand where your insecurities are coming from. But if he becomes defensive or angry when you try to talk about the affair, it's a tell-tell sign he is still struggling with his emotions for his lover and wishes to avoid the topic.
