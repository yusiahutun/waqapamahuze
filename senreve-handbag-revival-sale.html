Dropping a few hundred on a designer handbag is truly a big investment, especially since discounts on luxury pieces don’t come around often. But there are many perks to selecting a designer bag, such as higher-quality materials, longer shelf-life and a more impressive brand name. If you’ve been finding it hard to choose which one to go with, this might help narrow your search: Senreve’s Handbag Revival Sale just dropped.

This is the second year in a row the luxury brand is putting on the sale event, during which you can shop both new and gently used bags for up to 70 percent off—no, this is not a joke. Senreve wants to ensure no stock goes unused by taking a “firm stand against common luxury practices to burn or destroy bags,” unlike some other companies out there (we won’t name who.)

This is a rare opportunity to snag a piece of their wardrobe without paying full price.
