10 of The Spookiest Castles to Visit in the UK

1

Chillingham Castle, Northumberland

Gannet77//Getty Images

Taking the top spot is Chillingham Castle in Northumberland, famed for its action and battles. Shrouded in mystery, a visit here will be sure to make your spine tingle.

Tombola say: "This Northumberland castle is famous for its ghostly goings on, with 255 reviews mentioning the word 'ghost', from stories of a Pantry Ghost, who begs visitors for water, to men’s voices singing in the chapel, there is plenty of paranormal activity on offer for this Halloween."

2

Tulloch Castle, Dingwall

Over in Dingwall, Scotland, Tulloch Castle has heaps of stories to tell. Dating from the 12th century, it also scored highly for ghost sightings and ranked second in the study. Are you brave enough to give it a visit this Halloween?

3

Treasurer's House, North Yorkshire

lleerogers//Getty Images

"It was however Treasurer's House, in North Yorkshire, that has the most 'ghost' sightings," say Tombola. "Ranked 3rd on the list, it had the most reviews mentioning ghosts with 397 reviews mentioning ghosts."

4

Craig y Nos Castle, Swansea

While it's mostly used as a wedding venue, Craig y Nos Castle in Swansea is a Victorian-Gothic country house that was found to be filled with ghosts. Nothing beats a good ghost story on Halloween, so why not pay this castle a visit?

5

Alnwick Castle, Northumberland

darrenturner//Getty Images

Home to Harry Potter-inspired events, dragon quests and an exciting history, Alnwick Castle is also among one of the scariest places to visit this Halloween.

Tombola add: "Alnwick castle, ranked 5th in the list, had the most reviews ranked as 'scary'. 104 mentions of the word were found, from its historic stories of vampires and tales of the Grey Lady."

6

Muncaster Castle, Cumbria

Zoonar/S.Heap//Getty Images

Overlooking the River Esk, Muncaster Castle in Cumbria takes the sixth spot. Despite its breathing façade, it's a creepy destination that will make your wildest nightmares come to life.

7

Glamis Castle, Angus

hanskwaspen//Getty Images

A whole collection of ghosts have been seen skulking about at Glamis Castle in Angus, making it an excellent place to visit on October 31st. Make sure you don't go alone...

8

Dalhousie Castle, Midlothian

This magnificent 13th century castle set within acres of wooded parkland is the ultimate place to spend fright night – if you're brave enough. Taking the eighth spot, previous visitors explained they have seen ghosts here.

9

Berry Pomeroy Castle, Devon

Snowshill//Getty Images

Tucked away in a deep wooded valley, Berry Pomeroy Castle has been identified as one of the most haunted in UK. With a colourful history of intrigue, expect unusual sightings and sounds that will make your spine chill.

10

Ruthin Castle, Denbighshire

AlasdairJames//Getty Images

Ruthin Castle in Denbighshire might have taken the 10th spot, but it's still one of the spookiest in the UK. Lady Grey of Ruthin Castle is probably the most well-known ghost of the castle, although there are thought to be others roaming the rooms at night. Feeling brave to give it a visit?
