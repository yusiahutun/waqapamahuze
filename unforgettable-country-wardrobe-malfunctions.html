7 Unforgettable Country Wardrobe Malfunctions

Country wardrobe malfunctions are rare, and the recoveries are always first class. In this video of some of the most famous slips and splits, you'll see that even when mistakes happen, the most famous women and men in the genre know how to react with dignity and class.
