Genesis Lopez is a fitness model and popular Instagrammer, who gained tremendous fame when she started posting fitness pictures on her Instagram account Genesismialopez.

In her rise to becoming a fitness model, she worked as an athlete with VPX and BangEnergy and gained immense popularity on the internet.

Measurements (Height & Weight)

Genesis has maintained outstanding body measurements that every woman would love to have. She stands at the height of 5 feet 6 inches(1.70 meters) and weighs about 61kg.

Being a fitness model herself, she takes up the challenge of keeping her body in shape very seriously, even if it means following strict eating habits.

No wonder, she boasts such an attractive body figure.
