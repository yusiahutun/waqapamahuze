George Stephanopoulos enjoys the Fourth of July in face paint at Nantucket home

George Stephanopoulos jetted off to Nantucket for the Fourth of July, where he and wife Ali Wentworth spent time with some of their famous friends at the summer home of a Manhattan hedge fun manager and his wife.

The Good Morning America host was joined by Mariska Hargitay and her actor husband Peter Hermann as well as former Rachel Zoe COO Mandana Dayani and her producer husband Peter Traugott at the home of Jeff and Ashley McDermott.

George got in the holiday spirit too wearing a stars and stripes t-shirt and some face paint for the festivities. 

Squad: George Stephanopoulos and wife Ali Wentworth and Mariska Hargitay and husband Peter Hermann spent the Fourth of July in Nantucket

All made up: George and Ali posed for a group photo in face paint (above) and wearing stars and stripes t-shirts

Ladies: All the women at the party also posed for a photo, with Mariska seen holding her daughter Amaya (above)

All dressed up: 'Happy, healthy, safe #FourthOfJuly to you all #UncleSamMeetAuntMariska #IWokeUpLikeThis #ThisIsMyUsualHat #RedWhiteAndTrueBlue #HappyBirthdayAmerica' wrote Mariska on this photo

The Fourth was a family affair as well for all the couples, who posed for photos with their children. 

Mariska could be seen in one photo holding her adopted daughter Amaya while her husband Peter held their son Andrew. The pair also have a 10-year-old son August.

The Law & Order: SVU actress posted six photos to her Instagram account on Sunday, including one where she showed off a giant star painted on her cheek and another posing with the son of her hosts.
