Taylor Swift Wore a Cozy Jacket Thats Ideal for Winter Dressing

Taylor Swift has been giving us the best winter fashion, donning sheer stockings, long coats, fluffy outerwear, and knee-high boots. She continued her cozy trend last week while out to dinner with Selena Gomez by reaching for a plaid Miu Miu skirt, comfortable knit sweater, tall and sexy Reformation footwear, and a maple-toned Saint Laurent bag. But I would be doing you a disservice if I didn’t mention Swift’s cool-girl leather and shearling jacket.

Layered over her blouse, the singer’s top layer was from Gant. The statement piece featured a soft brown hue and offered an oversized, boxy fit. Look closely and you’ll see dark brown piping shooting across the shoulders, chest, and forearms. The downside? This applause-worthy piece has since sold out — and that’s not even accounting for the high $1,700 price point. Thankfully, I found a great lookalike for $200 on Nordstrom.

This Sam Edelman faux shearling aviator jacket comes in sizes extra small to large and includes that Swift-worn khaki color. You can additionally look forward to that same linear leather piping. The cozy collar can also be buckled up for additional warmth, while an interior fur lining spills onto the hems to tie the look together.

Similar styles are rapidly picking up steam online, racking up 40.4 million views on TikTok. In fact, I recently secured my own version from American Eagle. While it’s offered in brown, I opted for the black color, adding an edge to the overall look. Treasure and Bond offers a similar alternative, though this one is reversible. Wear it leather side-up for a sleek aesthetic or flip it inside out for a super snuggly option. 

Even though shearling leather jackets include two contrasting materials, they’re easier to style than you may think. Look to Swift’s preppy attire and slip on a mini skirt and boots or go casual and reach for relaxed-fit jeans, a slim turtleneck, and mini platform Uggs. You can also fully lean into textiles and snag a tweed skirt, tights, and heeled loafers. Whether you dress your Swift-inspired jacket up or down, you’re sure to be toasty all winter long.
