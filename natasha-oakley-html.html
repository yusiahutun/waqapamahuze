Natasha Oakley - Age, Family, Bio

Australian swimwear model as well as fashion and lifestyle blogger who co-founded the A Bikini A Day blog with Devin Brugman. She and Devin began designing swimsuits for their company Monday Swimwear. She has over 3.6 million followers on her popular Instagram account. 

Before Fame

She and her partner Devin were sleeping in the same bed for months before finding success online.

Trivia

In 2017, she traveled to the Amalfi Coast of Italy and posted pictures to Instagram of her trip frequently. 

Family Life

Born in Australia, she lived in Maui before moving to Los Angeles, CA where she launched her career. Her sister Sophia Vantuno is a blogger and photographer.

Associated With

Her A Bikini A Day blog features a Quotes to Live By section featuring quotes from Marilyn Monroe and Blake Lively, among others.
