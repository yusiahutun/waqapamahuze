    Is a microwave better than a toaster oven?

    After weighing the pros and cons, I believe that a microwave and a toaster oven both have their own unique advantages. While a microwave is great for quickly reheating leftovers or defrosting frozen meals, a toaster oven can be more versatile when it comes to cooking crispy toast or baking small dishes.
