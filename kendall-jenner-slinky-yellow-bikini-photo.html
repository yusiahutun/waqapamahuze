Kendall Jenner Is Striking in a Slinky Yellow Bikini

Kendall Jenner brought out a teeny textured bikini for a yacht day with her friends.

The supermodel wore a slinky yellow-and-black set from Casa Gamero, the swimsuit label founded by her friends, sisters Victoria and Sofia Villarroel. The bikini features a triangle top and a scrunchy adjustable thong bottom—both in a snakeskin-esque print.

Jenner shared a mirror selfie in the swim set on her Instagram Story yesterday, tagging the brand. The Villarroels have yet to release the exact set Jenner modeled, but the style will be available online soon, per the label’s official Instagram account.

courtesy of Kendall Jenner via Instagram

Jenner shared a video on TikTok later in the day, while enjoying some off-time on a yacht (likely the one she just bought herself) with friends Hailey Bieber, Lori Harvey, and Justine Skye. In the clip, Jenner wears a little white-and-blue top over her bikini and accessorizes with rectangular black sunglasses. She is seen pouring her girlfriends some of her 818 tequila.

For the funny clip, she used the viral Keeping Up With the Kardashians audio of Scott Disick and Kris Jenner. In it, Disick asks the momager, “Drink?” She responds, “You know, I’m a little—I would love …” and Disick interjects, “I don’t need a whole life story, just yes or no?” Jenner says, “Yes, a little baby one, thank you.” Disick laughs. “That was a—” he begins. “I was gonna give you a whole thing about how I was so nauseous,” Jenner says. “That was a lot,” Disick acknowledges with a laugh.
