Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen's fragrances are finally launching in the UK!

    Calling all Olsen twin fans - we have some news for you... After four (long, hard) years, Mary-Kate and Ashley's fragrance line is available in the UK!The range, titled Nirvana, become bestsellers when it launched in Sephora in the US in 2013, and the Nirvana Black and Nirvana White gaining cult status.

    Mary-Kate and Ashley have a hugely successful clothing line, Elizabeth & James

    Now, Harrods are exclusively stocking the entire Nirvana line, including Nirvana White, Black, Rose and Bourbon. As well as these original scents, the luxury retailer will also have two brand new additions - Amethyst and French Grey.

    Speaking about the UK launch to Vogue, Ashley said: "Since we launched Nirvana stateside more than three years ago, our team has consistently received emails from fans asking for Nirvana in the UK."We're excited to finally be able to share the fragrances with our customers here."

    The four Nirvana fragrances - Black, Bourbon, White and Rose

    She added: "Harrods is a London institution. We're pleased for Elizabeth and James Nirvana to be a part of what makes Harrods so special. "Discussing their hugely successful clothing and now lifestyle brand, Elizabeth and James, Mary-Kate said: "The last year has been an exciting time for us. We brought the apparel category in-house and opened our first retail store for the brand in Los Angeles. Expanding international distribution for the fragrances is another important layer to growing the brand on a global scale. "The two new fragrances, Amethyst and French Grey, are housed in the signature Nirvana packaging, an oblong bottle fashioned from textured glass with gold detailing. Mary-Kate told Vogue that the bottle was inspired by a vintage object.

    The two new additions to the fragrance family - French Grey and Amethyst

    The range is on sale in store and online now, with prices starting at £69 for 50ml and £95 for 100ml.

    "We're both very detail-oriented," Ashley added. "It was important that the design felt good in your hand. "As for the scents - Amethyst is a blend of tobacco, honeysuckle and cedarwood, while French Grey is a fresh, floral scent boasting notes of lavender, neroli and musk.
