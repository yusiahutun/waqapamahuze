Kenny Chesney Wants to Spread the Love With VIP Experience

Kenny Chesney is ready to spread the love when his The Big Revival tour begins on March 26. Enter here to win the ultimate VIP experience. Merch, pre-party access and upgraded tickets are just part of what you and your lucky best friend will enjoy at a tour stop of your choosing.

The Big Revival tour is hitting stadiums, arenas and amphitheaters across the country this spring and summer. If you win this Spread the Love VIP Experience, you’ll score early admission sandbar tickets or premium reserved tickets, access to the VIP pre-show party with a VIP-only bar and complimentary food, admission to Chesney’s Time Flies exhibit, merchandise, a parking pass (depending on which city you choose) and a VIP concert concierge.

To summarize, you'll be treated like the Chesney super fan you are — a king or queen amongst a crowd of of 20,000-plus screaming, singing diehards.

There are nearly a dozen ways to enter. Subscribe to Taste of Country’s YouTube channel or follow us on Instagram. Then subscribe to the email list, follow us on Twitter, test your Kenny Chesney smarts with trivia … you don’t have to do them all, but it will better your odds! Just one winner will be chosen from all entries when this contest ends at 11:59PM ET on April 26, 2015. Winner and guest will win the Spread the Love Experience for a show of their choosing that comes after that date.

All is not lost if you don’t win this contest, however, as you can still buy your way in. In fact, you can choose from the Seamless Experience or the This Is Our Moment VIP upgrade at the CID Entertainment website. Once again Chesney has partnered with CID to offer fans official VIP and travel package options. Prices and availability vary by city.
