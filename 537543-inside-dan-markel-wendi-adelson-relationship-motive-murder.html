Dan Markel's murder

Dan is no more. He was shot in his Tallahassee home on July 18, 2014. The Florida State Law professor was coming from the gym after dropping off his two boys at preschool. He was shot twice in the head in his car at point-blank range. He succumbed to the gunshot wounds 14 hours later.

Dan was murdered when he was in the midst of a contentious custody battle over their children at the time. After their divorce, Wendy wanted to relocate to South Florida with their two sons, but the judge denied her petition. After the incident, the Tallahassee police and the FBI embarked on investigations into the murder.

According to the prosecutors, the murder stemmed from the desperate need for the Adelson family to take him out of the way to allow Wendi to relocate with their two sons. So was Wendi Adelson charged? Wendi was not charged with the crime. But two of her family members, Charlie and Donna Adelson, were implicated in the crime.
