    Eleanor Calder, the other half of Louis Tomlinson

    Eleanor Calder is the girlfriend of One Direction star Louis Tomlinson. The 22-year-old and Louis were introduced in 2011 by a friend of Louis' bandmate Harry Styles. Eleanor grew up in London and studued at St Marylebone School. She later moved to Manchester to study politics and sociology at the University of Manchester, and as a student Eleanor worked for fashion store Hollister as a floor model.

    The brunette beauty recently graduated with honours and Louis flew in from Barcelona, where One Direction were on tour, to be her secret guest, helping her to celebrate with friends and family.Speaking to the , a source said: "He was a bit late for the start of the ceremony and he did not want to create a big fuss. He crept in and kept a really low profile. There were around 400 people there at the ceremony and hardly anyone clocked that it was him. "Despite not being a celebrity herself, Eleanor has hundred of fans and many have websites and Twitter accounts dedicated to her.

    But dating a member of One Direction, one of the biggest boybands in the world, has meant Eleanor has often found herself at the centre of unwanted attention from internet trolls.In 2013 Louis broke his silence on a Twitter campaign to get him back together with an ex-girlfriend, with a spokesperson telling the : "It's upsetting for Louis and Eleanor when Twitter campaigns like this start. I know some of the fans will think it's a bit of fun, but it's unnecessary when they are both very happy together."
