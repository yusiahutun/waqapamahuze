These Comfy Heeled Sandals Are My Go-To for Spring and Summer Weddings

Have you ever loved a pair of shoes so much you tell everyone you know about them? That’s exactly how I feel about the Jeffrey Campbell Bubblegum Platform Sandals. These shoes made me fall in love with heels, which I didn’t think was possible as a die-hard sneaker girl.

The eye-catching shoes come in several different colors that are perfect for spring, including neutrals like black and white and more adventurous options like lime green and red. They have a nostalgic, jelly-like texture that gets me compliments almost every time I wear them, a 2.5-inch block heel, and square toe. The best part? They’re super affordable at just $45, which means you don’t have to think twice about buying a color that’s more of a statement than a go-to; I recommend building a collection of several pairs like I did. 

I love the Jeffrey Campbell Bubblegum sandals so much that I own them in three colors. Every year when spring rolls around, I take all three pairs out of the back of my closet and keep them in heavy rotation until it gets cold outside again. They’re the perfect addition to a flirty or casual date night look since you can pair them with anything—they look fun and playful with jeans and tie together a sundress look. Plus, I’ve opted for them several times as a bridesmaid. Once the sun is out and open-toed shoe season kicks off, if I’m not grabbing a pair of sneakers, I’m grabbing these shoes.

With a platform heel and strong support around the foot, you don’t have to sacrifice comfort for fashion with these Jeffrey Campbell heels. Dancing the night away at a wedding feels exciting instead of dreadful—trust me, I’ve done it several times, and people are shocked that I’m not begging for flip-flops by the end of the night. The heel itself is chunky and stable and the foot support is impressive. I’ve never had to worry about foot pain when I wear these shoes; they're cute and reliable. What more can you ask for? 

Nordstrom shoppers are fans of the Jeffrey Campbell sandals, too. “I tried on the pink in store and was immediately shocked at how comfy they were,” one person said. “I instantly bought them and then proceeded to go home and buy two other colors,” they added. Another customer even said they “wore them for 12 over hours” without having to “stretch or break in the shoes.” The final consensus? They’re “really cute, flattering and surprisingly comfortable.”

If you’re eager to buy a pair (or two) for yourself, shop the Jeffrey Campbell Bubblegum Platform Sandals for $45 at Nordstrom.
