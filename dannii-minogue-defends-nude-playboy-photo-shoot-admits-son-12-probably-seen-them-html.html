Dannii Minogue defends nude Playboy photo shoot as she admits her son, 12, has probably seen them

Dannii Minogue has defended her 1995 nude Playboy photo shoot and admits even her 12-year-old son Ethan and his school friends have most likely seen the racy pictures.

She said she doesn't regret doing the saucy shoot and describes it as a 'complete feminist move'. 

'It was personally empowering to me. I still stand by those photos. I still think it's awesome,' she told The Guardian.
