Bethenny and Ramona' Debuts on 'RHONY' With A 'Soft Porn' Sneak Attack (Video)

“At 6? Yeah,” Bethenny responded sarcastically. “Diane Sawyer came to her school yard and actually approached her. It's very common in 6 year olds to bring up nudity.”

Maybe Ramona's still harboring some saltiness about Bethenny not telling her she got two dogs?
