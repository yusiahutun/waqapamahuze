Mike Mondo Hits Back at Netizens Criticising Relationship with New Bae: &quot;Come Face Me&quot;

  • Radio host Mike Mondo told off his critics and advised them to face him in person that insult him online
  • The media personality has been facing sharp criticism from the time he went public with his relationship and disclosed he was dating Shiko Nguru
  • The sentiments come days after his girlfriend's ex, YouTuber Rama, through his lawyer, sent Mondo a desist letter to stop posting his two children on social media

Renowned radio presenter Mike Mondo has hit back at online critics who have been on his case since he started dating content creator Shiko Nguru.

The media personality has been serving couple goals with his woman by posting beautiful moments he has had with her online.

This, however, has not settled well with many Kenyans online who have condemned Mondo for dating YouTuber Rama's ex-lover.

Mike Mondo's message to haters

Some wondered why Mondo went for a woman with kids with another man, while others questioned why he saw the need to constantly update the public about his love life.

In an interview with Mpasho, Mondo said:

"There is this thing of Twitter warriors, keyboard warriors. If you're man enough to say it online, why do you need a parody account? You've said it, and that's what you believe, and I have met you here. Come now tell me in person. Come tell me I'm silly, now if you tell me that, you're being a man," he said.

Mondo revealed that he once met someone who insulted him on X who later approached him to ask for a drink.

"I told him I will buy you one if you repeat what you said. I hate the fact that people hide behind the keyboard. You are the same person who will DM me and ask for KSh 10,000 because your house is being closed due to rent. Come say it to me as a man," he added.

Mondo on being pressured to marry

Elsewhere, Mondo once disclosed on air that his mom wanted him to bring someone home on his fortieth birthday.

The fine on-air personality shared that he kept getting pressure from people who were reminding him that he was turning 40.

His fans empathised with him while some gave their own experiences on how they dealt with the pressure that comes with turning 30 and 40 years old.
