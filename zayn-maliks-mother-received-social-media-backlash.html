Zayn Malik's mother receives social media backlash

Getty

Zayn Malik's mother has accidentally landed herself into some hot water with Little Mix fans. After posting a picture of some flowers she had been sent by her son and his girlfriend Gigi Hadid, Trisha Malik accidentally liked a negative comment about her son's ex fiancé, Perrie Edwards – and the singer's fans are not happy.

It all started when Trisha posted a photo of some flowers she had been sent from the couple, where she said: "From my sonshine and Gigi. Love them."

Getty

Her comments section then filled with negative comments about Zayn's ex fiancé Perrie Edwards, one of which Trisha 'liked'. When she started to receive a backlash for this, she explained that she had done it by accident.

"I honestly don't have anything against Perrie, I never have and I never will," she explained. "This is the reason I don't come on to social media, because everything you do always gets blown up into some big drama. I didn't like this comment, I must have pressed it buy mistake when I was going through them, so please don't say things about me that are not true."

It is not the first time the Instagram like has landed people in unnecessary trouble, and with eagle-eyed fans all over the social media site, we're sure it won't be the last.
