Meaning of Desde el Corazn by Bad Bunny

The song "Desde el Corazón" by Bad Bunny is an ode to his Puerto Rican roots and pride. It is a celebration of his journey from humble beginnings to becoming a successful artist, all while staying true to his origins. The overall theme of the song revolves around gratitude, perseverance, and a deep love for his homeland.One standout lyric is: "Ayer era bagger, hoy soy millonario" (Yesterday I was a bagger, today I am a millionaire). This lyric highlights the theme of personal growth and transformation. Bad Bunny acknowledges his past struggles and contrasts them with his current success. It serves as a reminder to never forget where one comes from and to appreciate the journey taken to achieve dreams.Another powerful lyric is: "Que este es Stephen Curry, me quieren los Warrior' pero na', yo sigo en los Cangrejeros, con los Capitanes y los Vaqueros" (They want me like I'm Stephen Curry, the Warriors want me, but no, I'm still with the Cangrejeros, with the Capitanes and the Vaqueros). This lyric portrays the theme of loyalty and staying true to one's roots. Regardless of external temptations or offers, Bad Bunny remains connected to his Puerto Rican teams, symbolizing his deep loyalty to his culture and community.The lyric "Y yo me quedo en Puerto Rico aunque vuelva María" (And I'll stay in Puerto Rico even if Maria returns) encompasses the theme of resilience and love for one's homeland. Bad Bunny expresses his unwavering commitment to Puerto Rico, emphasizing that even in the face of adversity (referencing Hurricane Maria), he remains dedicated to his country. This highlights his desire to support and uplift his people, showcasing the strength of his connection to Puerto Rico.The song also includes a reference to influential artists in the Latin music industry, such as Daddy Yankee, Tego Calderón, Don Omar, Wisin, Yandel, and more. This lyric serves as a testament to the impact these artists have had on Bad Bunny's career and his artistry. It symbolizes the theme of inspiration and gratitude towards those who paved the way for his success. It is a reminder that every artist is shaped by the artists who came before them, and they carry a responsibility to continue pushing the boundaries of their craft.Overall, "Desde el Corazón" encapsulates the essence of Bad Bunny's journey and the deep connection he maintains with his roots. It celebrates personal growth, loyalty, resilience, and gratitude. The song serves as a heartfelt declaration of love for Puerto Rico, honoring the island and its people, while also paying homage to those who have influenced him. Through its powerful lyrics and passionate delivery, Bad Bunny connects with his audience on a profound level, reminding everyone to cherish their origins and embrace the journey that leads them to where they are today.
