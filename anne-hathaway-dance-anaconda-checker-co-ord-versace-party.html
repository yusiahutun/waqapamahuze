Anne Hathaway Dances to Nicki Minaj's &quot;Anaconda&quot; at Versace Party

Celebrities really are just like us. If you need any proof, look no further than Anne Hathaway, who can’t resist a catchy tune.

At Versace’s celebration during Milan Fashion Week, the Eileen star was captured in a video posted online early Saturday busting out her best moves in the middle of a dance circle to the Nicki Minaj’s 2014 hit “Anaconda.” In the clip, all eyes are on Hathaway as she gets groovy to the bop like one of the most devoted Barbz, hitting every beat, dropping it low, and twerking, while reciting the sampled line “Oh, my God. Look at her butt.” The crowd around her exclaims and cheers as the pop culture icon takes her moment.

To make things even better, Hathaway wore the perfect ensemble for the impromptu dance party, donning a two-piece tank-and-miniskirt set completely covered in a black-and-white disco checker pattern. Each square of the retro design flapped about mesmerizingly as she shimmied and spun. Like the It girl she is, Hathaway paired the playful outfit with pointed-toe stilettos in the color of the season—candy-apple red—adorned with a bow at the front for a coquette touch. And to prove she was the coolest person in the faintly lit room, she wore narrow black shades, and finished it all off with a stack of gold bangles and large diamond stud earrings.

MEGA

Outside, Hathaway added a long black coat with gold buttons and a small matching croc-embossed purse. Fittingly, she let her rich brown hair loose, styling her silky tresses down and straight, while opting for dewy, glowy glam. We’ll be adding this look to the top of our Pinterest party mood boards, and this energy to the front of our minds throughout 2024.

Just hours before, the Devil Wears Prada star had ended her fashion drought at Versace’s Fall/Winter 2024 show, in an ensemble that was utterly fabulous but not made for dancing: a sizzling red leather dress that made its runway debut while she sat front row. The piece featured sultry off-the-shoulder straps, a corseted bodice, and a form-fitting midi skirt with folds of fabric across the waist, which she teamed with the same vibrant heels, leather bag, and shiny jewelry.

Women’s Wear Daily posted a candid video to Instagram that captured Hathaway discussing to her fiery frock, saying, “I can’t turn, but I can breathe.” She sure made up for that lack of mobility later in the night.
