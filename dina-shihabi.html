About

Dina Shihabi is a 34 year old Saudi Arabian Actress. Born on 22nd September, 1989 in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia and educated at Emirates International School and Dubai American Academy, she is famous for her roles as Amira Jafari in the 2014 romantic comedy film Amira & Sam, Hijriyyah Al Bouri in the CBS political drama series Madam Secretary, Neda Kazemi in the Netflix superhero series Daredevil, Hanin Suleiman in the Amazon Prime Video original political action thriller series Tom Clancy's Jack Ryan, Nour in the Hulu comedy-drama series Ramy, Dig 301 in the Netflix original cyberpunk science-fiction action series Altered Carbon, and Melody Pendras in the Netflix supernatural horror thriller mystery series Archive 81.
