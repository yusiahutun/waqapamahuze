Kendall Jenner just put a festive spin on naked dressing

As a millennial supermodel, Kendall Jenner is no stranger to naked dressing, and this weekend she proved that it can be done in a festive way. The simple crepe mini dress, which is still available to buy, has embellished straps and a glitzy strip at the bottom, which gave Jenner's look a festive finish. She added even more glamour to the ensemble by teaming it with a pair of clear Amina Muaddi heels, which resemble Cinderella's glass slippers.
