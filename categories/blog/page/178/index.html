10 babies leap into the world at Genesis BirthCenter, Davenport

published on July 10, 2024
Genesis BirthCenter, Davenport witnessed an extraordinary event on Leap Day Thursday, with 10 babies making their historic entrance into the world, according to Genesis. Genesis BirthCenter Manager Kim Nimrick shared that the BirthCenter typically sees about 150 births in February, averaging four to five a day. But Leap Day was a whirlwind of activity with 10 births. “Having 10 births in a day is already a busy day for us.
