Lucy Dickins Named Global Head of Contemporary Music and Touring At WME

published on March 29, 2024
LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — WME announced that veteran talent agent Lucy Dickins has been named as the agency’s Global Head of Contemporary Music and Touring. In her expanded role, Dickins will oversee all aspects of WME’s Contemporary Music and Touring business across multiple offices, including Los Angeles, New York, Nashville, London, and Sydney. Kirk Sommer will continue in his role of Global Co-Head of Contemporary Music and Touring, while Becky Gardenhire, Joey Lee, and Jay Williams will continue in their roles as Co-Heads of WME’s Nashville office, overseeing country music touring for the agency.
