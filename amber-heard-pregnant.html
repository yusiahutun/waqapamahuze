Amber Heard and Johnny Depp Reportedly Expecting First Baby

Could a baby be on the way for Amber Heard and Johnny Depp? The couple, who will be celebrating their one-year wedding anniversary in a few weeks, are rumored to be expecting their first child together. According to reports from In Touch Weekly (so yes, it's very much still a rumor at this point), Heard is allegedly pregnant with a little baby Depp on the way. The source revealed,"They're keeping it a secret until she's safely out of her first trimester and starting to show. They're very private." 

This would be the first child for 29-year-old Heard and the third child for Depp, who already has two kids, Lily-Rose (16) and Jack (13) with Vanessa Paradis.
