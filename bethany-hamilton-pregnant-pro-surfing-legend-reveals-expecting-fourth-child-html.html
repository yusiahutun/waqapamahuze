Bethany Hamilton is pregnant! Pro surfing legend reveals she is expecting fourth child

Bethany Hamilton has revealed she is pregnant with her fourth child, with a sweet surfing video on Tuesday.

The professional surfer, 33, shared the happy news in an Instagram video of her on a surfboard featuring her husband Adam Dirks and their three children all surfing on one board.

Wearing a pink and blue bikini, Bethany - who is already mom to sons Micah, 2, Wesley, 4, and Tobias, 7 - proudly displayed her blossoming baby bump.

Captioning the family video, she wrote a lengthy caption where she expressed her joy at becoming a family of six.

She penned: 'We’ve got SIX of us on board!!! Adam and I, and our whole fam, are so excited to be growing our family! I believe that life is a gift, and each and every life has value.

Bethany is best known for a shark attack at age 13 that nearly derailed her career.

In 2003, while she was surfing off the coast of Kauai with a friend's family, a 14ft tiger shark bit off her left arm just below the shoulder.

The others were able to swim her back to shore and put a tourniquet on, but she had already lost 60 percent of her blood by the time she arrived at the hospital.

A tiger shark that was caught and killed shortly after the attack had pieces of a surfboard lodged in its jaws, and it was later determined to have been the attacking shark.

Despite her devastating injury, Bethany took less than a month to recover before returning to surfing, and she had her first professional contest after the attack in 2004. 

That same year, Bethany published the memoir Soul Surfer: A True Story Of Faith, Family, And Fighting To Get Back On The Board.

It was adapted into the 2011 film Soul Surfer, starring AnnaSophia Robb, Dennis Quaid and Helen Hunt, with Bethany serving as AnnaSophia's surfing double.

'I choose him everyday. I believe love can be a choice beyond just a “falling in love feeling,"' she wrote.

'The beauty that our marriage has become was worth all the boundaries, which could be considered sacrifices, I set before I was married through the dating time period.
