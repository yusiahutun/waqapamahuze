UFC ring girl almost bursts out of top as Insta follower leaves lewd sexual request

UFC beauty Carly Baker was subjected to a lewd sexual request from a fan on Instagram after posting a photo of herself nearly bursting out of her ring girl top.

The 34-year-old was one of three ring girls who showed up for a sold-out UFC 286 last weekend at the London O2 Arena.

Taking to Instagram the day of the event, Baker posted a selfie from her dressing room showing off her excellent physique, complete with a tight top that left little to the imagination.
