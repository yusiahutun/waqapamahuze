Sheree Zampino Wiki, Bio, Net Worth, Married, Husband, Divorce, Kids

Sheree Zampino is one of those women who has come to the fore thanks to her marriage to a Hollywood star Fresh Prince of Bel-Air alum Will Smith. Although now divorced from Smith Zampino has parlayed her celebrity marriage into a successful television career in reality and fashion. That’s what we know about Zampino.

Sheree Zampino Wiki/Bio

Zampino was born as Sheree Elizabeth Zampino on 16 November 1967 in Schenectady New York. Her father Les Zampino worked for a theatre company and her mother worked for a financial company. When Zampino was a child her parents divorced and Zampino lived with her father.

At a very young age, she nurtured her passion for fashion. After graduating from high school she attended the Fashion Institute of Technology where she learned more about fashion and also studied business to help her make money from her passion.

Since her marriage to Will Smith pushed her into the daylight Zampino has managed to stay around her collecting several concerts in the process. She started exhibiting in 2012 when she was chosen as one of the women who played in the real Hollywood Exes VH1 series. A chronicle of the life of a group of women separated from their famous husbands who are trying to start a new find someone new and grow in their companies. The show lasted about 3 seasons and the last episode was aired on July 2, 2014.

Zampino appeared in 2013 Do Something Awards ceremony by One Tree Hill star Sophia Bush. In 2015 she joined Minay Matthews’ documentary-talk show cast on Minay Tv where she talked to actors and other celebrities at premiere films and other red-carpet events.

Since then Zampino has launched her own website dedicated to shereeelizabeth.com fashion – an online “boutique where you can find everything from head to toe”. Sheree also runs her own program Eat Pray Live on the Black Hollywood Live online TV network which prides itself on being the world’s first online TV network dedicated to African-American entertainment.

Sheree Zampino’s Net Worth

It is not known to the public how much Sheree got paid for her role in Hollywood Exes nor has she revealed how much she earns from her other projects. However, reliable sources estimate her net worth to be in the range of $1.5 million to $3 million.

Education

The actress followed her passion for fashion after graduating high school and enrolled in the Fashion Institute of Technology in 1984 completing a business program there. She was awarded a diploma in both business and fashion design from FIT two years later. Subsequently, she furthered her studies at the Fashion Institute of Design & Merchandising located in Los Angeles.

What does Sheree Zampino do?

Zampino has been an active and successful figure in the entertainment industry since 2006 when she founded her skincare company, Whoop Ash. Since then she has gone on to become a well-known actor television personality, and fashion designer as well as a social media influencer. Her love for fashion is evident through her entrepreneurial venture with Whoop Ash and continues to be seen throughout all of her work today.

In 2009 Sheree Elizabeth launched an online fashion and accessories boutique. Since November 2017 she has served as the Chief Executive Officer of the company and is responsible for providing customers with a variety of jewelry pieces and clothing items.

Sheree Zampino Married/Husband, Divorce

Zampino was married and divorced twice. After a long relationship with Will Smith, the couple tied the knot in 1992. According to sources she met during the filming of “Fresh Prince of Bel-Air” by Smith. As a couple, Will and Sheree participated together in red-carpet events.

In 1995 about three years after the wedding the couple filed for divorce. Two years later in 1997, Will Smith married Jada Pinkett Smith while Sheree remained unmarried until 2007 when she tied the knot to a former NFL player Terrell Fletcher’s reverse pacemaker.

Fletcher performed with her at Holywood Exes. The Couple was married until July 2014 when they parted. They then applied for a divorce finding irreconcilable differences. Zampino asked for alimony but it was not clear how much she received from the former San Diego Chargers running back.

Despite Sheree’s divorce from Will Smith they both remained friends and have since supported each other. Zampino jumped to Smith’s defense in 2016 when actress Alexis Arquette posted a malicious message on Facebook claiming Will Smith was gay. This was after Jada Pinkett Smith called on Afro-Americans to boycott the Oscars.

However, Smith’s ex-wife Sheree did not waste her time firing at the actress. She posted a video on Facebook explaining the real reason for their divorce and why Smith is not gay.

“My marriage ended not over any infidelity. I didn’t find Will in bed with a man and I didn’t find him in bed with a woman. It just didn’t happen! Will was straight in 1992 and he’s straight in 2016. That just kind of is what it is. However, our marriage ended because we were so young. We were the same age then that our son is right now. It’s just a lot of responsibility. Marriage isn’t easy, especially at that age. Will was becoming bigger and bigger with his career. I’m becoming smaller and smaller. I just didn’t have the tools. We didn’t have the tools to make it work. I left because I was unhappy. It’s really that simple.”
