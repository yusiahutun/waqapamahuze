Josina Anderson: Everything You Need To Know About This Journalist!

Born to Lloyd Anderson and Yasmin Anderson on 15th of August, 1978, in Washington D.C., Josina currently ages 39. She grew up with her brother, who is now involved in a career of producing films.

Talking about the studies of Josina, she is a graduate of University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. She was even a track and field star during her enrollment at the university and earned a gold medal at the AAU Junior Olympic as well.

Her interest in athletics indeed seems to have driven her to become a sports reporter. However, she has also performed as a ballet dancer with the American Youth Ballet before and has completed residencies with the Dance Theatre of Harlem and Pennsylvania Ballet.

Furthermore, Josina has the experience of working as a reporter for Fox 31 in Denver. She has then joined Showtime's Inside the NFL and has also been successful to receive an Emmy for her impressive story entitled 'A Premonition to Addis Ababa' in 2009. Later, she got opportunities working with reputed channels like ESPN and Fox Sports.

But besides all her professional credits, she has been keeping her salary and other account details unreservedly to herself.

Furthermore, Josina, who reports on the happening news, has herself been a part of several news headlines now and then.

In September last year, she ended up going bald after losing Mark Sanchez QB bet, as Trevor Siemian got named the Bronco's starting quarterback instead of Mark.

 

This year, her tweet against the President of the United States, Donald Trump caught attention, where she asked President Trump to stop attacking the journalist.

Well, she has even faced a crucial situation after she got ESPN into trouble for reporting the showering habits of Michael Sam, the NFL's first openly gay player. It was indeed a scenario, where she was in threats to get suspended from the network, as the then coach of Rams; Jeff Fisher was livid regarding her reports.

Moving on to the love life of Josina, she remains unmarried and is yet to introduce a husband in her life. Moreover, to everybody's great surprise, she doesn't even seem to have any dating affairs; there are no sources that have claimed of her being involved in any romantic relationships with a boyfriend.
