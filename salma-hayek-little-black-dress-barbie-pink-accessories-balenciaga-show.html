Salma Hayek Completes Her LBD Look with Barbie Pink Accessories

The audience at Balenciaga’s Fall 2024 Show saw a lot of star- and style-power—among them was Salma Hayek, who struck in a little black dress whimsically accented with perfect pops of bubblegum pink.

The Frida actor sat front row to watch the fashion house unveil creative director Demna’s new collection of activewear, grunge, and eveningwear designs in sunny Los Angeles on Saturday.

Stefanie Keenan

Hayek looked glamorous in a long-sleeve, figure-hugging dress that hit just below the knee and featured ruching and an asymmetrical neckline. She made her look fun and playful with a bright pink Hourglass purse and matching Knife pumps, both by the presenting brand. “This is my Balenciaga Barbie,” she said of her accessories to Variety.

To make things even more luxurious, Hayek wore silver rope earrings by Balenciaga, black rectangle-framed shades, and several silver rings, including a glittery band that featured a huge red jewel. She added a final touch of pink with rosy glossed lips and styled her brunette waves into a high ponytail.

Araya Doheny

Last month, the actor-made-producer stepped out in a stunning red-carpet look for the 2023 Baby2Baby Gala. Shining in sequins, Hayek looked gorgeous in a sweeping emerald green gown by Bottega Veneta that featured a halter neckline and decorative tie. She glammed up with eyeshadow in the same earthy hue and brought some bling in the form of silver drop earrings and several statement rings.

Hayek was honored that night with the Giving Tree award, an award that was bestowed in recognition of her commitment to improving the lives of children in need globally.
