De Gea: Don't think your irreplaceable, Van Der Sar tells Man United keeper

Retired Manchester United goalkeeper Edwin Van Der Sar has fired a warning down De Gea's way and told the club's current number one he is not irreplaceable.

David De Gea seems hesitant to put pen to paper and extend his stay at Old Trafford, which means he could potentially walk out as a free agent come January 1.

"The decision needs to come at a point. It takes a long time now, so its a case of yes or no, but I'm not a director of United," the ex-United keeper told Skysports.
