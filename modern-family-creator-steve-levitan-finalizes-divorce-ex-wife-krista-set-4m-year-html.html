Modern Family creator Steve Levitan finalizes divorce with ex-wife Krista set to get $4m a year

Two years after Modern Family creator Steve Levitan's estranged wife Krista filed for divorce, it's now finalized with both parties walking away with millions of dollars.

The lengthy divorce battle has come to an end, according to TMZ, who reported Krista is set to get at least $4 million a year from various sources of income, including Modern Family.

As long as this money keeps coming in, Steve, 56, won't have to pay Krista anything in 'spousal support,' and Krista will also likely profit handsomely in many other ways.

Costly split: Modern Family creator Steve Levitan has finalized his divorce with his ex-wife Krista (seen here in 2015), who will get $4 million a year in the split.

TMZ also reports that Steve and Krista will split a number of bank accounts worth tens of millions of dollars, while additionally divvying up a number of cars and other properties right down the middle.

The exes are also slated to split the profits of their Santa Monica home they're selling, which is reportedly worth $40 million.
