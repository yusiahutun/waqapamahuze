Man becomes Peter Lovenkrands' dog for Halloween in one of all-time great costumes

One football fan has "won" Halloween 2022 with a sensational outfit that parodied a tweet made about former Rangers star Peter Lovenkrands' dog.

In 2016, Peter Lovenkrands shared a tweet that had a link to an Instagram photo on his Twitter account with the caption: “12 years old today. Happy birthday my beautiful boy.” And one unfortunate fan responded without opening the link.

He replied: "Hopefully grows up and scores goals against Celtic, just like his old man!” However, the link showed a picture of a Lovenkrands' dog - leading Gers fans to joke the dog would be playing for Rangers, including one infamous edited snap showing the dog celebrating a goal in a Rangers shirt.
