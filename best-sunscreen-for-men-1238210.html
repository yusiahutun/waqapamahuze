    What to Look for in the Best Sunscreens for Men?

    Men's skin also gets damaged by the sunrays. That’s why picking the ideal sunscreen that suits your skin type is important. Here are some key factors to consider while choosing men's facial sunscreen:

    • Get Higher SPF Protection: SPF or Sun Protection Factor measures how well a sunscreen can protect the skin. For everyday use, an SPF of 30 is usually sufficient. SPF 50 may be good for those spending a lot of time under the sun.
    • Look for Moisturizing Elements: Men's skin often become dry. So it is always better to choose sunscreens with moisturizing ingredients like aloe vera or vitamin E.
    • Vote for Non-Greasy Formula: Go for a sunscreen with non-greasy formula that never leaves any oily residue on your face. 
    • Opt for Natural Ingredients: For those concerned about chemicals, it is better to go for sunscreens with natural ingredients like zinc oxide.
    • Go for Water-Resistant Qualities: In case you sweat a lot or regularly take a dip in the swimming pool, go for the water-resistant sunscreens. You can rely on these products even when you skin is wet.
    • Must Have Anti-aging Properties: A few sunscreens for men come with anti-aging properties like antioxidants that prevents aging signs. For youthful skin, it is mandatory to apply sunscreen every time you go out in the sun.
    • Check Compatibility with Skin Type: Understanding your skin type and getting a men's sunscreen that complements it is vital. Whether you have oily, dry, or sensitive skin, there are products designed to suit your specific needs.

    The best sunscreen for men's face is more than just a barrier against the sun. It's a carefully selected tool that fits your lifestyle, skin type, and personal preferences. When you choose a good sunscreen, it doesn't only protect. But it also nurtures, hydrates, and compliments your skin. So, pick yours wisely!

    How Should You Apply Sunscreen?

    Applying sunscreen is essential to protect the skin from harmful UV rays. Here's an easy-to-follow guide on how to apply sunscreen:

    • Select the Suitable Product: Before you apply, choose the best men's sunscreen for your skin type and activity. Look for broad-spectrum protection, suitable SPF level, and water resistance if needed.
    • Time It Right: Just like following an everyday skin care routine, it is important to apply sunscreen on time. Apply it at least 15 minutes before stepping into the sun for optimum protection.
    • Understand the Amount You Need to Apply: A common mistake is applying too little. Take at least 1 ounce of a men's sunblock for the entire body to ensure adequate coverage.
    • Apply All Over: You need to apply sunscreen evenly to all exposed skin areas. Don't forget your ears, back of your neck, the skin around your eyes, and your feet. 
    • Reapplication Is Necessary: Sunscreen wears off. Reapply it every two hours or immediately after swimming, sweating, or towel drying. Regular reapplication is key to maintaining protection.
    • Use Application Tools: When using the spray sunscreens, hold the nozzle close to your skin and spray generously. Go for a makeup sponge to ensure an even application.
    • Protect Your Lips: Lips can also get tanned. Pick a lip balm with a good amount of SPF to protect them from the sun rays.

    From selecting the right product to covering the forgotten nooks, each step is important for a complete sun protection. Follow the steps we have mentioned based on our observations and your skin will be grateful to you!

    FAQ's

    What is the best sunscreen for everyday use?

    For everyday use, broad-spectrum, water-resistant sunscreen with SPF 30.

    What type of sunscreen works best for sports or swimming?

    For sports or swimming, use a water-resistant sunscreen with SPF 50 as it can protect your skin for longer.

    Do men need a different type of sunscreen than women?

    Men and women may use the same type of sunscreen with a very good SPF level if it suits their skin type.

    How do I choose the right SPF for my skin type?

    Choose SPF 15 for dark skin, SPF 30 for medium skin, and SPF 50 for fair skin, depending on your skin tone, type, and suitability.

    Should men wear sunscreen every day, even on cloudy days?

    Yes, men should wear sunscreen every day. UV rays can penetrate clouds, so protection is necessary, even on cloudy days.

    How much sunscreen should I apply?

    You need only 1 ounce of sunscreen for your entire body. Reapplication of the same is important every two hours or after swimming or sweating.

    Can sunscreen help prevent aging and wrinkles?

    Yes, sunscreen can help prevent aging and wrinkles since it can put a barrier to UV rays.

    Should men wear face sunscreen?

    Yes, men should wear face sunscreen if they want to maintain healthy skin.
