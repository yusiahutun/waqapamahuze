Gwen Stefani joins list of iconic Angels fans as singer enjoys fun family night out

Former The Voice US coach and musician Gwen Stefani has joined the list of iconic Angels fans as the singer enjoyed a fun family night out with her husband Blake Shelton.

The Hollaback Girl music star is all over social media after Angels fans spotted her at a baseball game this week. Videos of Gwen Stefani and Blake enjoying a fun night out watching the game have circulated on Twitter and turned their family interaction into a viral moment.

Gwen Stefani seen at Angels baseball game

It was a game night for the Shelton family on Monday (July 17, 2023). Gwen and Blake were in the tribune watching Los Angeles Angels and New York Yankees at Angel Stadium.

Their family interaction was captured in a social media video after a viewer spotted the celebrity couple when the camera zoomed into the audience.

The singer was looking down at her phone when she spotted the cameras and waved in excitement to viewers at home, which was shared on Twitter. Gwen seemed to have a lot of fun and she officially joined the list of iconic Angels fans.

Gwen’s fans get excited about baseball

The viral moment captured on cameras led to plenty of reactions on Twitter and many of the musician’s fans stated that they are into baseball now.

“She’s part of the team now?” one fan questioned on Twitter. “What can’t she do?”

“The one time I’m excited about baseball, thanks to The Sheltons,” someone else commented. “Love seeing them having family night, living their blessed life together!

“She should come to every game. What a game,” another person wrote.

Someone else noticed a subtle detail about Blake in the viral footage and added: “Blake is all chill and eating his popcorn.”

Gwen released her new single

The Don’t Speak singer debuted her newest single, True Babe, in June. She rocked a pink Barbie-inspired bodysuit to promote the release of the song, a month before the much-awaited release of Greta Gerwig’s live-action film.

Her admirers couldn’t stop commenting on her stunning look on Instagram and Gwen regularly makes headlines for her timeless beauty and youthful appearance.

“She still looks 30,” one fan commented, while another wrote: “For a 53-year-old you sure do look younger.”

Someone else commented on a video that featured Gwen without any makeup and added: “You look so good and so young wearing less makeup!”
