Early life

The TV host was born on January 21, 1966, in Minneapolis, Minnesota, United States. Unfortunately, she is private and has not revealed much about her childhood. However, she grew up in Minneapolis, raised by a journalist father and a housewife mother.

Growing up, Connelly wanted to become a model. She also picked up her passion for cooking from her mother, who taught her how to cook. She was interested in acting and music, especially rock music, which was a hit then.

Connelly attended a local high school in Minneapolis. After graduating, she enrolled at St. Michael's College, pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Journalism.

Career

What does Kate Connelly's career life look like? After graduating from college, she briefly worked as a model, one of her childhood dreams. However, after a short stint, she gave up the career to pursue another one of her passions – cooking, which she combined with the journalism degree she had obtained.

Connelly's passion and journalism skills led her to the quirky television show Robin Reach Talking Food, which aired on the Television Food Network. She was the host of the show, where she cooked on-air while dealing with several hilarious mishaps in the kitchen, such as flying champagne corks and running a blender without a lid.

Connelly welcomed guests to the show, which included sommeliers, restaurateurs, chefs, and ordinary people. When Robin Reach Talking Food ended, she did not return to TV hosting again. However, she became a co-owner of a few restaurants, which she had opened with her then-husband, Bobby.

Aside from TV hosting and cooking, she has enjoyed a brief acting role on the big screens. One of Kate Connelly's known movies is Blaze, where she appeared alongside Chris Hemsworth.

Dating history

Is Connelly married or dating anyone at the moment? The former TV host keeps a low profile and has shared little about her personal life. Her last known relationship was with her ex-husband, Robert "Bobby" William Flay, a renowned celebrity chef and restaurateur.

Bobby Flay and Kate Connelly met when he appeared as a guest on her TV show, Talking Food, in 1994. At the time, Bobby had just gone through a divorce and was living alone in an apartment. Connelly, on the other hand, was a single mother juggling her career and raising her son.

Bobby and Kate were instantly attracted to each other, and he invited her a few days later for a date at one of his restaurants. They started dating and got engaged a few months later. The couple then tied the knot on October 1, 1995, at one of Bobby's restaurants in New York.

Bobby and Kate's wedding ceremony was attended by around 100 guests – their families and close friends. Amongst the guests were famous chefs such as Tom Valenti and Mario Batali – Bobby's friends.

Sadly, the marriage did not last long as the couple split in 1998 after around three years of dating. For Kate, this was her first divorce. However, this was Bobby's second divorce (having been married to Debra Ponzek until 1993. He would go on to date and marry actress Stephanie March (Law and Order star) after divorcing Kate. The marriage also ended in divorce in 2015.
