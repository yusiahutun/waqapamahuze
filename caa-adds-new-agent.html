CAA Adds New Agent

Ruth Gonzalez, Mitch Rose's assistant at CAA for the past two years, has been promoted to agent. Artists that she works with as a shared responsible agent are Flickerstick and Handsome Devil (with Brian Greenbaum, for him she worked with prior to Rose) and COLD (Darryl Eaton). The specific territory Gonazalez will be booking has not yet been determined.
