John Krasinski and Emily Blunts Complete Relationship Timeline

John Krasinski and Emily Blunt are pretty much Hollywood royalty, and they’ve been going strong ever since they were introduced by mutual friends all the way back in 2008. Through the years, they’ve made a move across the country together, filmed two blockbuster horror films, and welcomed two adorable daughters. Nearly 15 years into their marriage, they still can’t get enough of each other, and we couldn’t be more obsessed.

If you find yourself to be similarly obsessed, read ahead to see the couple’s entire relationship timeline—from their very first pizza date to their latest red carpet appearances.

January 2024: The pair swap outfit colors for back-to-back red carpets

To kick off 2024 (and awards season), Blunt and Krasinski prove they’re still the cutest couple around when they wear red and ecru outfits to the 81st annual Golden Globe Awards and then swap colors a week later at the 29th Critics Choice Awards.

For her first appearance, Blunt wore a spaghetti-strap Alexander McQueen dress with a flowy sheer skirt and a bodice adorned with decorative ivory and gold sequins. Meanwhile, Krasinski chose a burgundy tux with wine-colored pants a black bowtie.

Lionel Hahn//Getty Images

A week later, the pair do a little switcheroo, and Blunt wears a one-shoulder Armani Privé dress composed of ruby red sequins, while Krasinski chooses a custom cream tux from Dolce & Gabbana, which he paired with chocolate slacks.

Gilbert Flores//Getty Images

September 2023: Daughters Hazel and Violet make rare public appearance at US Open

Krasinski and Blunt typically keep their daughters out of the public eye, but the family makes a rare appearance at the US Open in September, where daughter Hazel intently watches the game, while Violet spends much of the match in her daddy’s lap.

Throughout the championship game, Blunt is seen excitedly cheering as she sports a patterned bodycon dress and glasses. Violet matches with her mom in her own floral patterned dress and sparkly headband, while Hazel dons a black t-shirt and a Boston Red Sox hat. Krasinski is seen in a ribbed royal blue polo.

Gotham//Getty Images

July 2023: Emily announces break from acting

Blunt stops by the Table for Two podcast, where she reveals that is taking a year off from acting to spend more time with her family.

“I worked quite a bit last year and my oldest baby is 9—like we’re in the last year of single digits—and I just feel there’s cornerstones to their day that is so important when they’re little,” she says. “And it’s, ‘Will you wake me up?’, ‘Will you take me to school?’, ‘Will you pick me up?’, ‘Will you put me to bed?’ And I just need to be there for all of them. For a good stretch. And I just felt that in my bones.”

February 2023: The couple walk their first red carpet together since the pandemic

While the early years of their relationship were marked by numerous red carpet appearances, Blunt and Krasinski remained relatively out of the spotlight for a few years, until their made their joint return for the Screen Actors Guild Awards in February 2023.

For the event, Blunt wears a bright red bandage dress from Oscar de la Renta that is adorned with cherry blossom appliqués that wrap around the gown. Her hubby, who stands by her side all across the carpet, opts for a black pinstripe suit from Dolce & Gabbana.

Amy Sussman//Getty Images

July 2022: Emily and John let their kids watch dad on screen for the first time

Although Krasinski hasn’t acted in too many kid-friendly roles up to this point, he finally let his daughters watch one of his films where he voiced the role of Superman in DC League of Super-Pets.

He talks about the experience on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon, saying that his daughters “loved it.” He reveals, “They were laughing very very hard. I think up until now they didn't actually believe I was in the business, because they've never seen anything I've done.”

May 2021: A Quiet Place Part II has its wide release

After a number of delays due to the COVID-19 pandemic, A Quiet Place Part II, starring both actors, has its wide release in theaters, arriving on Paramount+ 45 days later. It becomes the first film of the pandemic to cross the $100 million mark for domestic theatrical releases.

December 2020: Emily discusses their marriage on their 10th wedding anniversary

A few months after their 10th wedding anniversary, Blunt discusses her and Krasinski’s marriage (and their pandemic life) in an interview with People.

“Having John's support is everything, because we are each other's confidant,” Blunt says. “That shared understanding has really been a very vital anchor for me.”

She expresses her gratitude for getting more time with her kids as well, saying, “Being around little ones during the pandemic was such a saving grace because they would just be bouncing around the house, and your job is to protect them from what is happening and make sure their life remains joyful. That was a pretty great lifeline for John and me during the pandemic, for sure.”

March 2020: A Quiet Place Part II has its world premiere

The Hollywood couple celebrate the premiere of their second film together at the Rose Theater in Lincoln Center in New York City. For the event, Blunt wears a leather red dress from Alexander McQueen, with a heart-shaped neckline, a belted waist, and a frilly lace bottom. Meanwhile, Krasinski brings his own pop of color with a french blue suit.

That same month, the U.S. goes into lockdown for the COVID-19 pandemic, halting the wide release of the Quiet Place sequel.

Paul Bruinooge//Getty Images

July 2019: The couple announce their second film together

After the über success of A Quiet Place (which grossed over $340 million worldwide and received praise from critics), Blunt and Krasinski announce their second project together: A Quiet Place Part II.

June 2018: The couple snag $11 million residence in Brooklyn Heights

After living in Park Slope, Brooklyn, for a few years, Blunt and Krasinski snag two condos at the Standish building in Brooklyn Heights (which are on the same floor, indicating they likely combined the two apartments into a spacious abode).

According to the Observer, the residences in this building are characterized by their bay windows, custom kitchens with Bertazzoni Italia and Bosch appliances, brass Fantini fixtures, and bathrooms equipped with rain showers and travertine and mosaic accents.

March 2018: A Quiet Place premieres at South by Southwest

A year after announcing the project, Blunt and Krasinski walk the red carpet at the South by Southwest film festival, where A Quiet Place gets its world premiere.

For the event, Blunt wears a baby blue striped gown that twists at the center, leaving cutouts at her hips. Meanwhile, Krasinski wears a black checkered zip-up top and matching slacks.

Matt Winkelmeyer//Getty Images

March 2017: Emily and John announce their first project together

Despite keeping their careers separate up to this point, the couple announce they will be working together on a new horror film, titled A Quiet Place, which will be directed by Krasinski, who will also star, alongside Blunt.

Krasinski teases the collaboration on Instagram, with a photo of him and Blunt, that is captioned: “Question: ‘John, who's the one actor you've been wanting to work with?’ Answer: (see above).”

Later on, the couple reveal to The Hollywood Reporter how their collaboration came to be. Krasinski says he had originally intended on asking Blunt to portray the role of Evelyn Abbott, but then: “I decided the safest thing to do was just have this experience on my own...I didn’t want this to be the one job that she was like, ‘Listen, I don’t know if I love this, but I love you, so I’ll do it.’”

But Blunt, who was preparing to film Mary Poppins Returns, read the script on a plane and knew she had to be a part of it. “I went sort of gray,” she says to THR. “I couldn’t imagine the thought of letting someone else play the part.” And so, she asked her husband if she could play the role. “It was like she was proposing to me,” Krasinski beams. “It was one of the greatest moments in my career. I screamed out, ‘Yes!’ I’m surprised we didn’t emergency land in San Antonio.”

June 2016: The pair welcome their second daughter

Krasinski takes to Twitter yet again to share the news of their second daughter’s arrival. He makes the post on July 4, writing, “What better way to celebrate the 4th... than to announce our 4th family member!!! 2 weeks ago we met our beautiful daughter Violet #Happy4th.”

August 2015: John opens up about his marriage and says he is Emily’s no. 1 fan

In an interview with People, Krasinski still sounds like he’s in the honeymoon phase as he discusses his marriage to Blunt.

“It's really that we just got lucky,” he says. “It's one of those things—I'm having more fun now than the day I met her, and it’s pretty wild. I don’t know the formula to it, but I think she’s hilarious, extremely talented, and I’m definitely her no. 1 fan.”

Over a year after welcoming their daughter, Hazel, Krasinski notes how that has changed their lives too. “I think when you commit your life to anything like that it changes everything,” he says. “All of the cliches are true.”

May 2014: John shares first photo of daughter Hazel

Ever the excited dad, Krasinski shares the first photo of Hazel on his Twitter account, writing, “Pics of kids should only come direct from parents. So I'm thrilled to introduce you guys to Hazel!!! #NoKidsPolicy.”

February 2014: Emily and John welcome baby Hazel

On February 16, Krasinski reveals to the world that he and Blunt have welcomed their first child. “Wanted to let the news out directly,” the star wrote on Twitter. “Emily and I are so incredibly happy to welcome our daughter Hazel into the world today! Happy bday!”

September 2013: Emily reveals she’s pregnant with their first child

In an exclusive interview with Us Weekly, Blunt’s rep reveals that she and Krasinski are expecting their first child together. “They both want kids, it's one of the reasons they got a bigger place, in a neighborhood that you can raise a family,” the source explains. “They both couldn't be more excited.”

During this time, Blunt starts filming her Disney musical flick Into the Woods, in which she plays the Baker’s Wife (who is trying to get pregnant). She later recounts the experience to Playbill magazine, saying, “At the time [of filming], I was pregnant. I hadn't had my daughter yet, but it was kind of extraordinary being pregnant and playing this woman, who was desperately yearning to be pregnant, and feeling like I was the luckiest person in the world… In a way, it gave me a deeper insight into this woman and her yearning to be a mother. Those maternal instincts were kicking in for me. It was kind of a trip, being pregnant and hiding behind every tree in the woods by the end of my pregnancy!”

July 2010: They tie the knot in Italy

After less than two years together, the lovebirds tie the knot at George Clooney’s estate in Lake Como, Italy, with an intimate ceremony attended by their closest family and friends.

During an interview with Elle, Krasinski reveals that Clooney offered up the house very enthusiastically. “George said, ‘I have this place and you should feel free to use it,’” Krasinski recalls. “Only on the fourth ask did I say yes, because the first three times I thought, There's no way he is serious. But I started to see his feelings get hurt. I actually hurt George Clooney’s feelings.”

For the event, Blunt wears a custom Marchesa gown, along with a three-carat round diamond and platinum ring from Neil Lane. However, while she surely looked fabulous, the actor later revealed on The Late Late Show With James Corden that there was one thing she wished she could change.

“I got a bad spray tan, and I would probably change that,” she confesses. “I look at the pictures and it just has an orange hue that is unnatural to normal skin color.”

She adds, “It was a do-it-yourself—why was I on a budget on my wedding day? Why? It was a bit patchy. It stinks! And it was a very hot day, so if you sweat and you have a spray tan—wearing white. Just seeping orange. It was terrible.”

Celebrity attendees at the ceremony–other than George and Amal Clooney—include Meryl Streep, Matt Damon, and Jimmy Kimmel.

October 2009: They make their first public appearance together

Blunt and Krasinski make their first public appearance together as a couple, only a mere few months after their engagement, at the 16th annual Women in Hollywood Tribute hosted by Elle.

John Shearer//Getty Images

For the event, Blunt wears a strapless nude mini dress with a belted bow around the waist and a set of matching strappy heels. Meanwhile, Krasinski wears a black blazer and slacks with a white button-up shirt and a purple striped tie.

Stefanie Keenan//Getty Images

August 2009: The pair get engaged

After less than a year together, Krasinski’s rep confirms the pair’s engagement to People in August 2009, with a statement that simply says, “We can confirm that John and Emily are engaged.”

The details of the proposal are hush-hush, but Krasinski tells Access Hollywood, “It was very casual and I was nervous, and the whole nine yards.” He also mentions that the proposal took place in L.A. and both he and Blunt cried. (If it was anything like Krasinski’s proposal while playing Jim on The Office, we bet it was romantic.)

January 2009: They get chummy at the SAG Awards

Although they arrive separately, both Krasinski and Blunt attend the 2009 SAG Awards, and the pair are seen getting chummy backstage, like in one photo where they laugh during a conversation with Sean Penn.

Lester Cohen//Getty Images

November 2008: Emily and John meet at a restaurant

Prior to their connection, Krasinski was in a relationship with his Office co-star Rashida Jones. Meanwhile, Blunt had been dating Michael Bublé for a few years, until they separated in 2008. That fall, while Krasinski was dining at a restaurant, he was introduced to Blunt by a mutual friend.

Blunt recounts the story during a visit to Late Night with Seth Meyers, where she says, “It's kind of a sad, lame story. I was in a restaurant, he was in the restaurant. I was sitting with a mutual friend, and my friend Gray goes ‘Oh my god, that's my friend John.’ And that was it. He was sitting with our friend Justin Theroux, and he abandoned Justin and came over to us. He just stood there and made me laugh.”

In an interview with The Hollywood Reporter, the two argue about who asked who out first. “Probably me, I think,” Blunt says, before Krasinski counters, “Yeah, right.” The two go back and forth for some time until Krasinski says, “It was me asking for a while and you took some time, and then we finally had a date.”

But despite whoever actually asked for the first date, the pair ended up having pizza at Krasinski’s apartment in West Hollywood, and the rest was history. When speaking of evening, the pair choose to keep some details to themselves though, as Blunt teases, “It’s so precious, I don’t want to talk about it. Is that all right?”
