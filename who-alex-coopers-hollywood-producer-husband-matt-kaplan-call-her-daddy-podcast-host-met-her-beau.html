What does Matt Kaplan do for work?

According to Elle, Kaplan works in the entertainment business. After working as an assistant at Lionsgate and Blumhouse, he landed a gig at Awesomeness Films (the studio behind Netflix’s To All The Boys I’ve Loved Before) where he eventually became the president of the company, according to the magazine.

These days, 40-year-old Kaplan reportedly runs Ace Entertainment as well as Trending, a media company he launched with Cooper that’s signed deals with influencers including Alix Earle and Madeline Argy.

How did Alex Cooper meet Matt Kaplan?

Cooper told her and Kaplan’s love story to Vogue Australia, saying it all began in July 2020 when she was doing “back-to-back meetings” with film and TV producers on Zoom. “I got on my last meeting of the day with Ace Productions, Matt’s company,” she told the publication. “I got on Zoom and immediately started thinking, wow, this producer is very handsome – but, stay focused Alex. There were about five other people on the Zoom from his company, but I kept just staring at Matt’s video square.”

Although the TV show didn’t work out, Cooper had a strong feeling that it wouldn’t be the last time she’d see him. “We went on a few dinner dates in LA and sat in parking lots, pandemic-style, and there was no doubt in my mind after the first dinner that he would be the man I was going to spend the rest of my life with,” she told Vogue Australia. “I’m extremely picky so this was big for me.”

Who is Matt Kaplan’s ex-wife?

Before finding love with Cooper, Kaplan was married to his now-ex-wife Claire Holt for a year after tying the knot in 2016, according to People. Holt is an Australian actress who’s starred in TV shows including The Vampire Diaries and The Originals, per her IMDB. Kaplan also dated one of the Olsen twins, Ashley, from 2001 to 2004, Nicki Swift reports.

Who made Alex Cooper’s wedding dress?

With the help of her stylist Mimi Cuttrell, Cooper chose the dress of her dreams – from Danielle Frankel. “The dress truly turned out to be my dream,” the bride told Vogue Australia. “I felt so classic and chic in it. I felt confident and sexy while still elegant and timeless. [From there] the rest of the looks fell into place.”
