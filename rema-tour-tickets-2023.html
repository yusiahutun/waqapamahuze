Rema tour 2023: How can I get tickets?

THE RISING Afrobeats star is set to spend summer 2023 performing for his fans.

After kicking off in May 2023 in India, Rema will travel to various locations throughout North America and Europe as part of his Rave & Roses World Tour II.

How can I get tickets for Rema's tour?

On April 24, 2023, Rema announced the North American and Indian legs of his tour on his Instagram page, @heisrema.

On May 16, he shared additional summer festival dates with his 5.1 million followers.

Fans can purchase tickets via Live Nation through Rema's website or on Ticketmaster.

Where will Rema be performing?

Rema will continue his Rave & Roses World Tour II on May 28, 2023, at the Afro Nation music festival in Miami, Florida.

Who is Rema?

Rema — whose real name is Divine Ikubor — was born on May 1, 2000, in Benin City, Nigeria.

The 23-year-old is an Afrobeats and "Afrorave" singer, as well as a rapper.

Most read in Celebrity

He signed with the Nigerian record label Jonzing World — whose parent company is Mavin Records — in 2019.

Rema first found fame through his hit song, Dumebi, which was released in 2020.

His success has since continued to grow, and on March 25, 2022, he released his debut album, Rave & Roses.

In August 2022, he collaborated with Selena Gomez via the remix of his track, Calm Down.
