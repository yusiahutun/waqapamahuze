About

Australian Singer Olivia Newton-John was born Dame Olivia Newton-John on 26th September, 1948 in Cambridge, Cambridgeshire, England, UK and passed away on 8th Aug 2022 Santa Ynez Valley, California, USA aged 73. She is most remembered for her iconic role as Sandy Olsson in the 1978 musical romantic comedy film Grease and for her chart-topping hits “Physical,” “Have You Never Been Mellow” and “You’re the One That I Want,” her Grease duet with John Travolta. Her zodiac sign is Libra.

British Musician Andy Gibb was born Andrew Roy Gibb on 5th March, 1958 in Manchester, England and passed away on 10th Mar 1988 Oxford, England aged 30. He is most remembered for I Just Want To Be Your Everything. His zodiac sign is Pisces.
