Inside Khloe Kardashians incredible body transformation: after being compared to sisters Kim and

Ever since entering the public eye in 2007 after the premiere of Keeping Up With The Kardashians, Khloe Kardashian’s weight has always been the centre of attention. Her sisters Kim and Kourtney had similar physiques while Khloe was taller and fuller.

Over the years her weight has gone up and down in front of the world; her lifestyle journey on full display for all of us. Now, she works out, eats clean and appears to be comfortable in her body. So, what is her secret after all these years?

1. Quit the emotional eating

The Revenge Body with Khloe Kardashian businesswoman has admitted to having baby fat as a young girl, according to US Weekly. She said her weight had always been a battle, admitting that her body was always compared to her slimmer sisters, Kim and Kourtney.

US Magazine reports the star said she was an emotional eater, apparently turning to food during her parents’ divorce in 1991. She said she and her brother Rob were always heavier and food was given to them to “keep us company. All we did was eat crap”.

2. Find purpose

In an interview with Jay Shetty on his podcast On Purpose in 2019, Kardashian said her big moment away from the yo-yo dieting culture came when she split from husband Lamar Odom in 2013. She said she was unhealthy and overweight for most of her life and it was during that time that she turned to the gym for a release: “I needed a release and I needed to feel strong and I just needed to get rid of all these thoughts.”

3. Go slow

As her body transformation continues to wow the world, Kardashian’s biggest tip is that she learnt to understand that long-term results don’t come fast. She told US Weekly she had tried so many diets and all the weight would just come back because it was unsustainable.

US Magazine reports she said her biggest victory came after she lost her weight slowly. In an interview on Poosh with her sister Kourtney, she said that even though her weight loss journey has been slow, it’s been “rewarding”.

4. Keep evolving

From lifting weights to Pilates and boxing, Kardashian has made sure she keeps her exercise regime exciting all the time. When she was doing Pilates, she told NewBeauty that she could see a visible difference in her body. She recently shared a picture of her back muscles, showing the difference between three months of training.

Her coach, Joël Bouraïma, has posted images of Khloe training, as well as her sisters Kim and Kourtney. He is also motivating; in a post showing off Kourt’s physique, he said it was a moment to be celebrated.

5. Understand limits

Although Kardashian changed her diet, she admitted that she makes sure she focuses on training hard so that she can indulge if she wants to. According to Insider, the reality TV star said the key was to set boundaries and understand limits when it comes to going off track. Kardashian is open to having pizza and chocolate, according to US Weekly, but makes sure it is not all the time, and then makes sure she works out hard.
