Christopher Cody: Meet Billy Ray Cyrus' Son

Christopher Cody, renowned as the eldest son of famous singer and actor Billy Ray Cyrus, remains an enigma in Hollywood. While his surname might ring bells of stardom, Cody seems to want no part in it. Hence, he gained media attention thanks to his father’s fame, half-sibling, and pop sensation, Miley Cyrus. 

Like her father, Cody’s half-sister, Miley, has been a household name in the industry. She is a megastar who became famous following her “Hannah Montana” role and well-known songs. Despite his renowned father and half-sibling, Cody has decided to maintain a low-profile lifestyle. Consequently, unlike the rest of the Cyrus clan, he lives far from the limelight.

Here’s a look into the life of the celebrity child.

Who Is Billy Ray Cyrus’ Son?

Billy’s oldest son, Christopher Cody Cyrus, was born on April 8, 1992, to Billy Ray Cyrus and Kristin Luckey. Cody’s mom, Luckey, dated the iconic singer briefly while working as a  waitress in South California. However, the lovers had separated at his birth, and Billy was expecting another child with his new girlfriend, Tish. 

Consequently, mere months after his birth, the world welcomed his famous half-sister Miley, who later became a Disney sensation with “Hannah Montana.” She followed in her father’s footsteps and established herself as a successful singer, songwriter, and actress.

However, unlike his wealthy half-sister, Cody’s earnings are more modest. His means of livelihood is one of the many differences between the siblings. Despite his family’s widespread acclaim, Christopher Cody lives a low-profile life.

What Does Christopher Cody Do for a Living?

Unlike the illustrious world of the Cyruses, where the glitz of Hollywood prevails, Billy Ray Cyrus’ son has consciously opted for a more private life. According to online reports, Christopher Cody comes from Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, where his mother worked. He attended Myrtle Beach Intermediate School and Forestbrook Middle School in South Carolina.

Afterward, he attended the University of Texas, where he studied Law. While all his Cyrus half-siblings are into the glamor and entertainment world, Brandi, an actress; Trace, a musician; Miley, an actress and musician; Braison, a model; and Noah, an actress; Christopher Cody, works in an Electronic Store. 

Does Christopher Cody Have a Good Relationship With Miley Cyrus?

While the bond between Cody and Miley remains shrouded in mystery, instances like him attending her concert in 2007 indicate a certain level of camaraderie. In addition, Christopher Cody has spoken warmly of his half-sister. He praised her talent and described her as a lighthearted individual who enjoys a good laugh.

Billy Ray Cyrus welcomed Cody into the world with Luckey at the height of his fame. Even though the renowned musician established a life with Tish, Miley’s mother, he did not shirk his responsibilities towards his firstborn. Billy maintained a relationship with Cody, from child support to medical bills. Although Billy opened up about wanting custody of his oldest son, he and Luckey co-parented amicably. 

However, as time progressed, the father-son bond strained, making it challenging for Cody to connect with Miley and the rest of the Cyrus family. His absence from the family’s reality show and the lack of closeness with Billy certainly left scars. However, Cody has clarified that there’s no bad blood with family members.

Is Christopher Cody Married?

Yes, he is. Amidst the complexities of his family dynamics, Christopher Cody found solace in love. Marrying his high school sweetheart, Cody often shares glimpses of his family life on Facebook, painting a picture of contentment. However, due to his private lifestyle, the identity of his wife eludes the media. 

How Many Siblings Does Billy Ray Cyrus’ Son Have?

While he might be the only child of Billy and Luckey, Christopher Cody is surrounded by family, both from his mother’s side and the Cyrus clan. According to reports, his mother went on to have three other daughters with whom he shares a relationship. He also has three half-siblings on his dad’s side: Miley, Noah, and Braison. In addition, his siblings also include Billy Ray Cyrus’ stepchildren, Brandi and Trace Cyrus.

Christopher Cody Movies

Although he has not appeared in any movies, Christopher Cody has movie credits in his name as an editor. According to reports, Billy Ray Cyrus’ son is known for “Blindsided” (2017), “Cheerleader Chainsaw Chicks” (2018), “12 to Midnight” (2021), and “20-minute Orgasm” (2017).

Christopher Cody Cyrus is a testament to the fact that one can share the genes of celebrities but choose a life far removed from fame. Another celebrity child, like Christopher Cody, is actor Morris Chestnut’s son, Grant Chestnut. His story is a gentle reminder that normalcy and privacy can sometimes be more precious than fame.
