A Very Laurel Christmas Laurel Mercantile

It's no secret that Laurel is most magical during the holiday season. We jump through all the hoops and ring all the bells and whistles to celebrate this time of year!

Whether you're a local or just visiting during the holiday season, you're in luck! We've made a list of events and activities you absolutely can't miss in Laurel, MS this month. 

Tree Lighting & Annual Laurel Sertoma Club Christmas Parade

Friday, December 1, 2023  

Join us in Pinehurst Park as we light Laurel's tallest Christmas tree in downtown with carols and candlelight from 5:30-6:00 pm.

Bring the whole family to experience the largest parade in the state of Mississippi at 7 pm in Downtown Laurel. Enjoy watching the floats, listening to the bands and as we all anxiously await for the arrival of Santa!

Pancake Day

Saturday, December 2, 2023

A decades-long tradition for many families is to enjoy pancakes at the YWCO or First Baptist Church on the first Saturday in December. Laurelites young and old return home for this event, and the highlight is bumping into friendly faces while in line or finding a seat.  The griddle starts cooking at 7:00 am and runs to 1:00 pm. Tickets are $10 for an all you can eat for a delicious pancake breakfast!

United Way's Prancer Path

Located in the Back Lot area of 250 Magnolia Street in Downtown Laurel, the Prancer Path is a beautiful display of holiday cheer. Trees sponsored and decorated by businesses, individuals, and schools in our community are featured in this fundraiser for the United Way of the Pine Belt. 

Christmas Lights at Landrum's Homestead

Every weekend in December 4:00-8:00pm

Step back in time and take a Christmas walking tour of the past. Enjoy wagon rides, hot chocolate, photos with Santa, funnel cakes and marshmallow roasting. Each night from 4:00-8:00pm the homestead will be illuminated with thousands of Christmas lights! Click here to learn more.

Charlie Brown Jazz Christmas Concert

Saturday, December 8, 2023

Don’t miss the 14th annual family-friendly Laurel Christmas tradition! The show includes a performance by The Joe Cool Trio – John Milham (drums), Chris Spies (piano), and Chris Severin (bass) - guest Clarence Johnson - all performing Vince Guaraldi’s Masterpiece Soundtrack, A Charlie Brown Christmas.

This year’s show will feature the musical gifts of American jazz trumpeter Kermit Ruffins. Click here to purchase your tickets. 

Cookie Stroll

Saturday, December 16

Join the businesses in Downtown Laurel for some CHRISTMAS MAGIC! This family-friendly event is an after hours, last-minute-Christmas-shopping recipe for Christmas joy.

Merchants will be open late, each offering their own interpretation of Christmas cookies, as well as deals, specials, and promotions that will help you finish off your Christmas list!

In addition, you'll experience live music in Trustmark Art Park, carolers across downtown, Christmas movies on the big screen at Wilson's Commissary, a live Nativity at The Rusty Chandelier, carriage rides by Cloverleaf Carriage, Cookies for a Cause at The Remnant, live readings of Christmas childrens' books, a Santa photo op, and MUCH MORE!

Shop Local 

We encourage all our visitors and guests to browse all of Downtown Laurel's stores and check the good boys and girls off your Christmas list this year by shopping at and supporting our local small businesses. 

We hope to see you in Laurel sometime in the month of December. It's sure to be a jolly time!

And one more thing...

HGTV's Home Town Christmas Special

December 17, 2023

Tune into HGTV on December 17, 2023 for the Home Town Christmas Special with Erin and Ben to celebrate the Holiday season.
