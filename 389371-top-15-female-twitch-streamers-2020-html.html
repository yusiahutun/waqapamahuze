1. KittyPlaysGames

KittyPlays, also known as Kristen, is a 26-year-old streamer from British Columbia. She has 800,000 followers and is one of the top 100 Twitch streamers. She streams two times in a day and focuses on popular games like CS: Go, Fortnite, and PUBG.

Kristen fans are fond of her because she never shies from interacting with them on her chat, and strives to maintain an upbeat and relaxed environment. She also doubles as a vlogger with a niche in cooking, travel and racing. That is why she remains to be the biggest female Twitch streamer in 2020.

2. LegendaryLea

Lea May Currier is the Twitch streamer who goes by the name LegendaryLea. She is not only a streamer but also a passionate gamer. She has built a name for herself because of the way she entertains her audience through a holistic and friendly experience.

Lea is a Physiology and Neuroscience graduate, but gaming is her true passion. She enjoys interacting with her followers while gaming and that is why she is so popular on the platform. The games she plays more are CS: GO and Hearthstone.
