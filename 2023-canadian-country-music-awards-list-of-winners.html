2023 Canadian Country Music Awards: List of Winners

HAMILTON, ON (CelebrityAccess) – The 2023 Canadian Country Music Awards took place Saturday (September 16), hosted by the duo The Reklaws, it was broadcast live via CTV, CTV.ca and the CTV app.

The star-studded lineup of performers included multi-platinum entertainer Dean Brody, 2x CCMA Award winner Jade Eagleson and the Canadian platinum-certified group James Barker Band, among many more.

Eagleson ended the night with three wins, including Entertainer of the Year. Tenille Townes took home her fifth win for Female Artist of the Year to tie him.
