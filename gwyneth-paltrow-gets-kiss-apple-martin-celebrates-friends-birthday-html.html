Gwyneth Paltrow gets a kiss from Apple Martin as she celebrates friend's birthday

She is the daughter of Gwyneth Paltrow's oldest friend.

And Deven Dees was honored with a shout out by the actress turned lifestyle guru on Tuesday, as she celebrated her birthday.

Sharing a picture showing herself with the teen, and her own 14-year-old daughter Apple Martin, Gwyneth wrote: 'Happy birthday to you, sweet Doobs. My other daughter, your giant heart lights up our lives.'

Mother's love: Gwyneth Paltrow gets a kiss from Apple Martin as she celebrates 'my other daughter' Deven Dees's birthday

Deven is one of five children - four daughters and a son - born to Daun Dees, Gwyneth's long-term confidant.

Daun was with Gwyneth at her bachelorette party last summer, and gets regular shout outs from the star on Instagram.

On Thanksgiving Gwyneth tagged her in a post thanking her close friends and family.

'I could not be more grateful for these people and those not pictured who bring love and laughs and heart to my life. @bradfalchuk @electricminky @jcvn40 @daundees @hilaryhaw #chrismartin #blythedanner and all of you!!! Happy happy Thanksgiving.'

Close friends: Gwyneth shared a picture of Daun as part of her Thanksgiving shout out last November

Close pals: California-based Daun, a mother of five who used to live in  Hong Kong, is one of Gwyneth's closest confidants

And on Daun's birthday two years ago, she wrote: 'Happy birthday my loving, supportive, optimistic, soulful, dear friend.

'I am blessed to be by your side, as we raise these girls alongside one another. You bring so much light into our lives, this photo says everything. We love you.'

While her friend has four daughters, Gwyneth, 46, is a mother of-two, with Apple joined by 12-year-old brother Moses.

Busy mom: Currently, the Goop founder is prepping for the release of her new cookbook, The Clean Plate, which came out on Tuesday

She is also a stepmother to two from her wedding to Brad Falchuk, with the Glee producer father to teenagers Brody and Isabella with ex-wife Suzanne Bukinik.

Currently, the Goop founder is promoting her new cookbook, The Clean Plate, which came out on Tuesday at $35.

She has been sharing images as she cooks up food from her own recipes in a taste kitchen to her social media account.
