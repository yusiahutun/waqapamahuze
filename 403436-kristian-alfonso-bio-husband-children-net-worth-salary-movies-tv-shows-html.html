Kristian Alfonso biography

Kristian was born on 5th September 1963 in Brockton, Massachusetts, US. She is the daughter of Gino Alfonso (father) and Joanne Alfonso (mother). She has one sister known as Lisa Alfonso.

She studied at Brockton High School. However, she has not offered any information about her university studies, such as the institute attended or course taken.

Career

Kristian-Joy started her career as a figure skater at a young age. She was a gold medal champion at the Junior Olympic Figure Skating Championships. Unfortunately, her career came to an end at the age of 13 after the tobogganing accident.

Later on, she joined the modelling and fashion industry. She had appeared on the cover of more than 30 magazines at the age of 15 years. Some of the magazines are Vogue, Glamour and Harper's Bazaar, among others.
