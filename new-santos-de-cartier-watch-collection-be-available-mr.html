New Santos de Cartier watch collection to be available on MR PORTER

Luxury maison Cartier has partnered with MR PORTER to launch a curated selection of seven timepieces from the brand’s Santos de Cartier collection, including an exclusive strap that is only available at the men’s style e-commerce platform. The watches will be available in pink gold, yellow gold, and stainless steel variations. 

The collection is available for purchase from April 5.
