Avril Lavigne Dons a Bikini Amid Lyme Disease Battle, Looks Amazing

Avril Lavigne is on the mend.

The "Complicated" singer, who has been battling Lyme disease, looked like the picture of health on Thursday while relaxing poolside in a bikini.

"Mind body sun and water," she captioned the Instagram snap above.

In the photo, the pop star wears a huge grin as she takes a dip in the pool while wearing an embellished bandeau top and a pair of dark sunnies.

The relaxing break is well deserved, following Avril's first performance in over a year at the 2015 Special Olympics last weekend.
