Sergio Busquets Children: Meet Sergio Busquets Son Enzo Busquets

Sergio Busquets is a Spanish professional footballer who was an integral member of the Spanish team that won the 2010 FIFA World Cup. Sergio Busquets plays and captains both Barcelona and the Spanish national team.

Sergio Busquets was born to a former reserved backup goalkeeper for Barcelona in the 1990s. Busquets plays as a defensive midfielder and sometimes plays as a central midfielder.

Sergio Busquets won the Bravo Award and the La Liga’s Breakthrough Player all in 2009. Sergio Busquets is aged 33 years and is regarded as one of the most underrated players of the 21st century.
