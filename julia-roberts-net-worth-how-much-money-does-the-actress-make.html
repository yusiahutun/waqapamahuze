Julia Roberts Net Worth: How Much Money Actress Makes

Pretty Woman made Julia Roberts the woman she is today, but it wasn’t the only highlight of her career. After the 1990 film was released, the Oscar winner went on to star in several other great films like 1997’s My Best Friend’s Wedding, Eat Pray Love in 2010 and her 2018 TV show, Homecoming. Thanks to her incredible talent, the actress has earned a whopping net worth. Scroll below to see how much money she makes. 

What Is Julia Roberts’ Net Worth? 

Julia has an estimated net worth of $250 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. The Georgia native made her acting debut in a 1987 episode of Crime Story. She’s come a long way since then, earning dozens more credits as an actress and producer in the years that followed. 

The Steel Magnolias star was even able to find love on set. She met her husband, cinematographer Danny Moder, while working on the film The Mexican in 2000. They got married two years later and welcomed twins Hazel and Phinnaeus in 2004. Their youngest son, Henry, was born in 2007. 

“There are great things I’ve accomplished in my career, and I’d be happy to accomplish more, of course, to impress my children and my husband. But you know what? I’ve been spoilt already,” the proud mom of three gushed to Harper’s Bazaar in October 2017. 

Julia admitted that becoming a mother changed the trajectory of her path in Hollywood. The icon confessed she used to be a “selfish little brat” who ran around “making films” for herself. Meeting Danny was a huge turning point for the Golden Globe winner, not only in her personal life but also in her career. 

“When I think about what makes my life my life, and make sense and just shine inside of me, it’s him,” she reflected on her marriage. “Everything has come from that for me.”

Julia’s Hollywood prowess has not slowed down in recent years. In 2022, she starred in the series Gaslit with Sean Penn and shared the screen with her longtime pal George Clooney in the romantic comedy Ticket to Paradise. 

How Else Does Julia Roberts Make Money? 

On top of her acting career, Julia runs the production company Red Om Films, responsible for producing Eat Pray Love, Maid in Manhattan and more. She has also been a popular face of the Lancôme brand for more than a decade, frequently appearing in their fragrance ads.
