Relationships are hard to maintain, much more when you are a celebrity. If your partner is a well-known celebrity and is not loyal to you, then your private life becomes a public spectacle. 

Falling under a similar kind of scenario is Ingrid Quinn, a social worker by profession whose husband acted like a bachelor when he was already married.

Have a look down at what actually had happened between the couple that led their marriage towards divorce.  

Ingrid Quinn’s Husband

Ingrid Quinn was married to David Boreanaz in June 1997. Ingrid met her husband, at an art gallery and started dating in 1994 when David was in the struggling phase of his career.

Ingrid Quinn photo with ex-husband, David Boreanaz (Photo: dianliwenmi.com)

Before meeting Ingrid, David had a reputation as a wild man, but that changed, and he became a gentleman after getting together with her. They both had a loving relationship and were struggling for a better life. Quinn even left her job to help manage her husband’s career.

However, Ingrid's married life hit a bump when her husband, started to live and act like a bachelor. According to the report, her husband got too close to his co-star on the show Angel and Buffy.

The situation got so serious that Ingrid started to make regular visits to David’s set to check on him. Unfortunately, following that, David also developed an addiction to Hollywood nightlife and was found hanging out with many girls. This behavior made Ingrid very upset and caused them to split up.

After the two years of married life, both of them called it quit in 1999. Ingrid Quinn and David Boreanaz filed a divorce in 1999, stating irreconcilable differences.

In 1999, while they were in the middle of their divorce, David stated to E! Online that Ingrid had always been a supportive partner who was always by his side and motivating him to do better. He said:

"Ingrid's always been supportive. She has the same attitude as I do: If you don't get something, it's not meant to be. She'll help me along the way, as far as preparing for something. She's tough as nails--one strong Irishwoman."

Quinn was always by her husband's side, which helped him come a long way. However, the couple didn’t even complete their five years together and ended their relationship. After two years of marriage, they got divorced and didn’t have any children together. 

After the divorce, Quinn never came into the spotlight and totally remained hidden from the media.

However, David Boreanaz married his fellow actress and model, Jaime Bergman, in 2001. After getting married to Jaime, he openly admitted cheating on his second wife, Jamie Bergman, in an interview with PEOPLE in May 2010, stating, 

“Our marriage has been tainted with my infidelities. I just want to be open and honest. I was irresponsible.”

David admitted cheating with a former mistress, who later contacted an attorney and threatened to contact media outlets. He stated, 

“I was associated with a woman who I was involved with and had a relationship with. She asked for money. I felt as though I was being blackmailed or there was some sort of extortion.”

Considering his cheating with Jamie, people speculated that Ingrid was also possibly cheated on by David during their time of marriage.
