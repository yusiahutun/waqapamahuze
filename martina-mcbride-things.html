10 Things You Didn't Know About Martina McBride

Martina McBride's catalog of hits is well-known, but a few facts about the singer may come as a surprise to even her biggest fans. In recent years, McBride has become one of the most accessible artists via Twitter, but she still holds back a few details of her private life. We promise not to embarrass anyone, but Taste of Country has dug up 10 things you might not know about the "I'm Gonna Love You Through it" singer.

The four-time CMA Female Vocalist of the Year has notched dozens of hit songs during her 20-plus year career in country music. "Concrete Angel," "Independence Day" and "This One's for the Girls" are three of her biggest.

ToC's 10 Things You Didn't Know About Martina McBride list includes facts about her music, family and superstitions. Read through to see how well we stumped you, and then feel free to share something other fans might not know in the comments section below.
