GALLERY: Sophie Grgoire-Trudeau's best ever hairstyles

    With just a few days to go before the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge embark on their royal tour of Canada, all eyes will be on the couple and their hosts Prime Minister Justin Trudeau and his wife Sophie Gregoire-Trudeau. Already renowned for her effortlessly chic style, Sophie has quickly become a beauty icon thanks to her elegant hair and make-up looks.

    As she prepares to welcome Prince William and Kate, we take a look back at Sophie's best beauty moments to date – click through for the full gallery… 

    All eyes were on the mother-of-three as she attended the Canadian Arts and Fashion Awards where she left her hair worn down in a sleek blow-dry style with a hint of volume at the roots. 

    Not only did Sophie wow in her dazzling golden gown for a state dinner in honour of the Mexican President, but she upped the glamour stakes with a slicked back updo, leaving the top section wavy to add a burst of volume. 

    Sophie was the epitome of elegance at a state dinner in honour of her husband in Washington, where she left her hair down in its natural curls, swept into a dramatic side parting for extra glamour. 

    The Canadian beauty pulled out all the stops as she and Justin met the Queen, with her hair swept back into an elegant chignon with waves at the roots for a hint of volume.

    The Canadian Prime Minister's wife mastered one of the summer's biggest hair trends when she chose to wear her hair back in a half updo, sticking to loose waves for daytime glamour as she and her family arrived in Hangzhou, China. 

    Not one hair was out of place as Sophie joined husband Justin for a visit to the White House, her glossy tresses swept back into a slick ponytail, the body of hair left in waves. 

    Although Sophie tends to favour loose, wavy hairstyles, she chose a super sleek and slicked back bun style as she headed to the Catalyst Awards, pairing the look with a statement red lipstick for ultimate wow factor. 

    The former television presenter proved the power of a great hair accessory when she added a chic fascinator to an eye-catching curly hairstyle for the opening ceremony of the Commonwealth Heads of Government Meeting at the Mediterranean Conference Centre. 

    The 41-year-old was the epitome of elegance with her hair left down in cascading curls, paired with barely-there make-up as she explored Hangzhou, China with her daughter Ella-Grace. 

    Sophie gave us major ponytail goals as she joined husband Justin at the Global Citizen Concert to end AIDS, opting to sweep her hair back into a loose wavy style for laidback glamour.
