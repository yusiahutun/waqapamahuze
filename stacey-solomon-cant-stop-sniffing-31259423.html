Stacey Solomon can't stop sniffing hair after using perfume that smells like 'heaven'

Stacey Solomon was excited to tell fans that her first hair product has landed online.

The television presenter invested in a small eco-conscious business called REHAB a month ago and has become their business partner.

Today, the haircare brand launched a new product to answer what Stacey was asking for — a hair perfume.

Stacey shared the news on Instagram, telling fans: "Remember I invested in a small business, REHAB. Well, when I first started talking to them, they asked me if I had any ideas for products for the business and I said is there any way of making a perfume that you can use on your hair?"

Speaking about the benefits of the product, she added: "Like a proper perfume, but not just that. It's something that going to nourish the hair, help it grow, help it maintain its healthiness."

The REHAB Hair Perfume, £38, is an ultra-hydrating product that adds a bouncy shine while working to repair damaged, dry ends.

The bottle is infused with rosemary and caster oil, just like this Forest & Shore hair growth oil, and is filled with the brand's signature sweet and soothing jasmine scent.

Stacey sprayed some on her tresses and sniffed. "This smells like heaven. Just imagine the [hair oil] capsules times 10," she said. I'm going to walk around with hair in my face like this all day. It smells so good and it feels so good, like it's an actual dry oil."

The 50ml bottle is developed to protect hair from harmful UV rays.

One fan reacted: "I need this so bad in my life! Jasmine has to be one of my favourite smells and to have something for your hair is just amazing!"

Another said: "Ordered can’t wait to try! My hair was so so soft and felt amazing with the hair oil capsules and I have just ran out so perfect timing!"

Arkive's The Good Habit Hybrid Oil is also a good hair oil using Baobab and Jojoba oils as main ingredients and is available at drugstore for £14.

Oribe's Gold Lust Nourishing Hair Oil has amassed 288 5-star reviews on SpaceNK and many customers have re-purchased despite the high price point.
